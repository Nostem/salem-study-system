# FP-AA-012 — Fire Brigade Staffing

> ⚠️ DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides requirements for fire brigade composition, minimum staffing, and allowances for temporary staffing reductions.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2022 Q93</div>
Minimum fire brigade staffing: <span class="hi-exam">5 qualified members</span> (per Salem and Hope Creek UFSAR 9.5.1.1.6). Fire brigade composition may be less than minimum for a period not to exceed <span class="hi-exam">2 hours</span> to accommodate unexpected absence, provided immediate action is taken to restore staffing. <span class="hi-trap">Do not confuse with 1-hour action times used in many Tech Specs — the fire brigade allowance is 2 hours.</span>
</div>
