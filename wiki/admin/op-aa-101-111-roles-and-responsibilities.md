# OP-AA-101-111 — Roles and Responsibilities of On-Shift Personnel

## At the Controls (ATC) Area Requirements

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q6</div>
<span class="hi-exam">At least one RO must be within Zone 1 (At the Controls / inner-horseshoe) at all times during Modes 1-6</span>, except for the time period required to acknowledge the receipt of an alarm outside this area or to perform actions required for safe plant operation. If one RO leaves the ATC area, the BOP operator remaining in the ATC area satisfies this requirement.
</div>

### Zone Definitions (Attachment 4)

- **Zone 1 — At the Controls:** Inner horseshoe area. At least one RO required at all times (Modes 1-6).
- **Zone 2 — At the Controls:** Extended control room area.

### Key Requirements

| Requirement | Applicable Modes | Source |
|-------------|-----------------|--------|
| At least one RO in Zone 1 at all times | Modes 1-6 | OP-AA-101-111 |
| SRO with command and control may NOT enter computer or equipment rooms | Modes 1-6 | OP-AA-101-111 |
| On-duty SM and CRS(s) shall remain within the Protected Area | All | OP-AA-101-111 |
| SRO shall remain within the Control Room area (may not go in opposite unit's computer/equipment rooms) | Modes 1-4 (per unit) | OP-AA-101-111 |
| STA within 10 minutes of Control Room Area | Modes 1-4 | OP-AA-101-111 |
| STA within 30 minutes of Control Room Area | Modes 5-6, Defueled | OP-AA-101-111 |

### SM Absence

When the on-duty SM leaves the Control Area, the Unit 2 CRS assumes Control Room Command and Control unless the SM designates a different SRO.

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam -- 2023 Q94</div>
Unexpected absence during shift change: per Attachment 2 Note 1, the shift complement may be <span class="hi-exam">one less than minimum for up to 2 hours</span> to accommodate an unexpected absence of an on-duty shift member. <span class="hi-trap">This does NOT permit any shift position to be unmanned upon shift change due to an oncoming member being late or absent.</span> The preferred action is to <span class="hi-exam">hold the off-going shift personnel until relief is available</span>. The on-duty RO cannot leave at shift change if no relief is available.
</div>
