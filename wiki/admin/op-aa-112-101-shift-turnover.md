# OP-AA-112-101 — Shift Turnover and Relief

> ⚠️ DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides requirements for shift turnover and relief, including evolutions that restrict turnovers.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2022 Q67</div>
Step 4.1.2: Shift turnover SHALL NOT be conducted during evolutions that may interfere with or distract operators, such as <span class="hi-exam">approach to criticality</span> and <span class="hi-exam">closing of the first generator output breaker during synchronization</span>. A diesel surveillance (running unloaded), SG level control in manual after sync, and a 10% per hour power ascension do NOT prohibit shift turnover.
</div>
