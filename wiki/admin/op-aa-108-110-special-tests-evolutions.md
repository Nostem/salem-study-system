# OP-AA-108-110 — Evaluation of Special Tests or Evolutions

## Purpose

Administrative procedure for screening, evaluating, and implementing special tests or evolutions that may involve significant risk to plant safety.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q96</div>
Per Attachment 2: an activity is classified as a special test or evolution when the activity is complex <span class="hi-exam">OR</span> infrequently performed (only one criterion needs to be met, not both). Per Step 3.4.1: <span class="hi-exam">Operations Shift Management approves IMPLEMENTATION</span> of a special test or evolution. <span class="hi-trap">Trap: the Senior Line Manager (SLM) ensures activities are screened and appoints a Special Test or Evolution Coordinator, but does NOT approve implementation — that is Operations Shift Management.</span>
</div>

### Roles

| Role | Responsibility | Source |
|------|---------------|--------|
| Senior Line Manager (SLM) | Ensures activities are screened; provides management involvement and oversight; appoints Special Test or Evolution Coordinator | OP-AA-108-110 |
| Special Test or Evolution Coordinator | Determines need for special administrative and management controls | OP-AA-108-110 |
| Operations Shift Management | Approves IMPLEMENTATION of special test or evolution | OP-AA-108-110 Step 3.4.1 |
