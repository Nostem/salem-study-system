# OP-AA-108-107-1002 — Salem and Hope Creek 500 KV Switchyard Operations Interface

## Purpose

Administrative procedure governing the interface between Salem/Hope Creek operations and the 500 KV switchyard, including notification requirements to ESOC (Electric System Operation Center).

## Key Requirements

- Per step 3.1.17, operations shift notifies ESOC when the generator Automatic Voltage Regulator (AVR) is NOT in Automatic mode
- Log the times when the station voltage regulation equipment is not in Automatic mode
- Also notifies the ER&T Generation Desk who will make appropriate notifications to PJM
- Per OHA H-27 ARP, 15 minute notification to ESOC is required
