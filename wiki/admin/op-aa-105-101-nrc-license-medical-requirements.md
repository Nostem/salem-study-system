# OP-AA-105-101 — Administrative Process for NRC License and Medical Requirements

## Purpose

Administrative procedure governing NRC licensed operator license administration, including medical examination requirements, NRC Form 396 restrictions, and license condition verification.

## Key Requirements

**NRC Form 396 — Medical Restrictions:**
- All medical restrictions listed on the NRC Form 396 must be satisfied when performing licensed duties
- Common restrictions include corrective lenses, hearing aids, and medication compliance
- An operator who cannot meet a medical restriction at the time of assuming licensed duties is NOT qualified to stand watch
