# RP-SA-102 — Containment Entries at Power

> DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Station administrative procedure governing containment entries while the reactor is at power (Modes 1-4). Establishes authorization requirements, dose control, and safety precautions for personnel entering containment during power operations.

## Key Actions / Information

- Containment entry at power requires authorization from the **Radiation Protection Supervisor (RPS)** (outside of Operations chain of command).
- Used in conjunction with SC.SA-ST.ZZ-0001, Salem Containment Entries in Modes 1 through 4.
- Survey maps must be current and reviewed for gamma and neutron dose rates.
- Personnel dose calculations must account for both gamma and neutron contributions.
