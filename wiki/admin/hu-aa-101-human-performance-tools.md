# HU-AA-101 — Human Performance Tools and Verification Practices

## Purpose

Administrative procedure governing human performance tools including peer checks, self-checks, pre-job briefings, and verification practices used during plant operations.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2018 Q78</div>
Step 4.8.6: <span class="hi-exam">"Peer checks for actions necessary to mitigate a transient are desired but not required."</span> During performance of IMMEDIATE ACTIONS in an abnormal procedure, peer checks are <span class="hi-exam">desired but NOT required</span>. <span class="hi-trap">Trap: candidates may incorrectly conclude that Peer Checks are required for ALL IMMEDIATE ACTIONS. The procedure explicitly states they are desired but not required during transients.</span>
</div>
