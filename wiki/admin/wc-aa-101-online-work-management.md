# WC-AA-101 — On-Line Work Management Process

## Purpose

Defines the process for managing on-line (at-power) maintenance activities, including roles and responsibilities for risk assessment, protected equipment, and FLEX capability.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q96</div>
Shift Manager responsibilities for on-line maintenance (Step 3.6):<br>
- 3.6.1: <span class="hi-exam">Ensures shift operations reassesses risk if an emergent condition results in a plant configuration not previously assessed</span><br>
- 3.6.2: <span class="hi-exam">Ensures shift operations apply Protected Equipment barriers IAW OP-AA-108-116</span><br>
- 3.6.3: Ensures fire risk management actions are implemented<br>
- 3.6.4: <span class="hi-exam">Ensures the site FLEX capability is maintained</span><br>
<span class="hi-trap">"Manages special or complex projects during on-line implementation" is NOT a SM responsibility — that belongs to the Online Maintenance Project Coordinator.</span>
</div>
