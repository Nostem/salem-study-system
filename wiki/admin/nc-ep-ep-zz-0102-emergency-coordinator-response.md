# NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0102 — Emergency Coordinator Response

## Purpose

Emergency plan implementing procedure for Emergency Coordinator (EC) response actions during declared emergencies.

## Key Requirements

- Accountability must be performed when a Site Area Emergency (SAE) has been declared
- Accountability must be completed within 30 minutes from the time of SAE declaration
- Assembly is performed when an Alert is declared (different from Accountability)
