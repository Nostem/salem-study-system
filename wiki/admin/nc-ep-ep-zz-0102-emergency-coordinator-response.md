# NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0102 — Emergency Coordinator Response

## Purpose

Emergency plan implementing procedure for Emergency Coordinator (EC) response actions during declared emergencies.

## Key Requirements

- Accountability must be performed when a Site Area Emergency (SAE) has been declared
- Accountability must be completed within 30 minutes from the time of SAE declaration
- Assembly is performed when an Alert is declared (different from Accountability)

## Key Requirements

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q99</div>
Emergency Coordinator (EC) responsibility transfers based on facility activation status:
<ul>
<li><span class="hi-exam">TSC NOT activated → Shift Manager is EC</span></li>
<li><span class="hi-exam">TSC activated, EOF NOT activated → Emergency Duty Officer (EDO) is EC</span></li>
<li><span class="hi-exam">TSC activated AND EOF activated → Emergency Response Manager is EC</span></li>
</ul>
<span class="hi-trap">Trap: authority is based on the individual's E-Plan position, NOT management title. The Site Vice President may fill the Emergency Response Manager position, but the authority comes from the E-Plan position.</span>
</div>
