# OP-SA-108-106-1001 — Equipment Control and Large Motor Starting Criteria

## Purpose

Provides requirements for equipment control, large motor starting criteria, and breaker reclosure policy, including emergency conditions under which breaker reclosure may be attempted without investigation.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q79</div>
Breaker Reclosure Policy (Step 4.2.2): if an <span class="hi-exam">emergency condition exists, as determined by the SM/CRS</span>, and a breaker fails to close, <span class="hi-exam">one attempt at reclosure may be made without conducting an investigation</span>, if that piece of equipment is essential for maintaining plant stability. <span class="hi-trap">Only the Shift Manager can authorize — not the Plant Manager. STA concurrence is NOT required.</span>
