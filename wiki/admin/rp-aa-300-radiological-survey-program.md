# RP-AA-300 — Radiological Survey Program

## Purpose

Provides requirements and guidance for performing radiological surveys, determining dose rates, and calculating stay times for work in radiation areas including containment entries.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2022 RO-A4</div>
Stay time calculation for containment entry: determine dose rate from radiological survey map for the work area, then calculate stay time = (dose limit / dose rate) x 60 min/hr. Must separately calculate <span class="hi-exam">gamma and neutron stay times</span> and compare against heat stress limits. The most limiting factor (lowest time) determines the maximum allowable entry time. At 22 CFCU area: gamma = 5 mrem/hr (24 min stay time), neutron = 40 mrem/hr (<span class="hi-exam">12 min stay time — most limiting</span>), heat stress = 15 min.
</div>

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2022 SRO-A4</div>
Containment entry dose calculation from survey map: <span class="hi-exam">gamma = 5 mrem/hr, neutron = 40 mrem/hr</span>. For 12-minute entry: gamma dose = (12/60) x 5 = 1 mrem, neutron dose = (12/60) x 40 = 8 mrem. Total dose per operator = 9 mrem. Must add to year-to-date TEDE for Administrative Dose Control Limit (2000 mrem) comparison.
</div>

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2019 RO-A3</div>
Dose calculation for Pipe Alley entry using Survey Map #1108410: highest On Contact dose rate = <span class="hi-exam">205 mrem/hr</span>, highest General Area dose rate = <span class="hi-exam">10 mrem/hr</span>. Per initiating cue, use On Contact rate as GA rate. Dose = <span class="hi-exam">205 mrem/hr x (1/60) hr = 3.42 mrem</span> (3.5 mrem rounded up is acceptable). Attachment 2 provides definitions of survey map symbols and abbreviations.
</div>

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2019 SRO-A4</div>
SRO version of Pipe Alley entry (Survey Map #1108410): same survey data but dose calculation uses <span class="hi-exam">2 minutes total (1 minute per operator x 2 operators)</span> at the On Contact rate of 205 mrem/hr. Total personnel dose = [205 mrem/hr x (1/60)] x 2 min = <span class="hi-exam">6.8 mrem</span> (6-7 mrem acceptable). Must also determine HRA authorization per RP-AA-463 (<span class="hi-exam">SRPT authorizes key release</span>).
</div>
