# OP-AA-300 — Reactivity Management

> ⚠️ DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides requirements for reactivity management during all reactivity changes, including roles and responsibilities for the Reactivity Management SRO.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2022 Q95</div>
Per Step 4.5 Note, the Reactivity Management SRO role may be filled by <span class="hi-exam">ONLY an active SRO license holder</span> (inactive SRO license holders are NOT qualified). Per Step 4.5.11, the Reactivity Management SRO is <span class="hi-exam">NOT allowed to perform peer checks</span> during reactivity manipulations — a second licensed individual must be positioned to perform peer checks. <span class="hi-trap">The Reactivity SRO and the peer checker must be different people.</span>
</div>
