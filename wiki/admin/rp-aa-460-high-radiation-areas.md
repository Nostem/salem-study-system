# RP-AA-460 — Controls for High and Very High Radiation Areas

> ⚠️ DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides radiological posting requirements and access controls for High Radiation Areas (HRA), Locked High Radiation Areas (LHRA), and Very High Radiation Areas (VHRA).

## Key Actions / Information

### Posting Thresholds

| Area Classification | Dose Rate Threshold | Source |
|-------------------|---------------------|--------|
| High Radiation Area (HRA) | > 100 mR/hr at 30 cm | RP-AA-460 |
| Locked High Radiation Area (LHRA) | > 1000 mR/hr at 30 cm | RP-AA-460 Step 4.2 |

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2022 Q72</div>
An area with <span class="hi-exam">1100 mR/hr</span> readings is a <span class="hi-exam">Locked High Radiation Area (LHRA)</span> (> 1000 mR/hr at 30 cm per Step 4.2). Per Step 4.2.5, access into a LHRA requires <span class="hi-exam">continuous</span> RP surveillance/coverage. <span class="hi-trap">Do not confuse HRA (> 100 mR/hr, periodic RP coverage) with LHRA (> 1000 mR/hr, continuous RP coverage).</span>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2019 Q98</div>
LHRA definition confirmed (SRO question): per RP-AA-460, an LHRA has a MINIMUM dose rate of <span class="hi-exam">1000 mrem/hr @ 30 cm</span>. <span class="hi-trap">Trap: 100 mrem/hr is the threshold for a High Radiation Area (HRA), not a Locked High Radiation Area (LHRA). Do not confuse the two.</span>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2019 RO-A3</div>
HRA entry requirements for Unit 1 Pipe Alley (84 ft Aux Building): per Radiological Survey Map #1108410, area is posted as <span class="hi-exam">High Radiation Area (HRA) and Contaminated Area (CA)</span>. HRA Briefing Sheet (Attachment 2) requires: lab coat, booties, rubber gloves. Dosimeter dose rate alarm setpoint is <span class="hi-exam">250 mrem/hr</span>. Accumulated dose alarm is <span class="hi-exam">10 mrem</span>. Response to alarm: leave area, close and lock door, notify RP. Check SRO every 15 minutes while in area.
</div>

<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2019 SRO-A4</div>

Highest On Contact dose rate = <span class="hi-exam">205 mrem/hr</span> (HRA threshold: > 100 mR/hr at 30 cm).
