# OP-AA-102-104 — Pertinent Information Program

> DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Establishes the administrative process for disseminating pertinent information to operations personnel through Standing Orders and Daily Orders.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2019 Q39</div>
<span class="hi-exam">Operations Management (Operations Manager or designee)</span> prepares and approves Standing Orders. <span class="hi-exam">Standing Orders</span> are used for temporary administrative requirements lasting longer than one day/weekend/holiday. <span class="hi-exam">Daily Orders</span> are typically valid for one day, weekend, or holiday period. <span class="hi-trap">The Shift Manager has responsibilities under the Pertinent Information Program but does NOT have authority to prepare and approve Standing or Daily Orders — that is Operations Management.</span>
</div>
