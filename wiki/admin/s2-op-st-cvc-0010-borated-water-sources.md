# S2.OP-ST.CVC-0010 — Borated Water Sources

> DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Surveillance procedure to verify borated water sources (RWST and BASTs) meet Tech Spec 3.1.2.6 requirements for level, temperature, and boron concentration. Performed in Modes 1-4.

## Key Actions / Information

### Attachment 2 — Borated Water Sources Data (Modes 1-4)

| Parameter | Instrument | Acceptance Criteria | Source |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| RWST Level | 2LT960, 2LT961, 2LT962, 2LT963 | 364500 to 400000 gals (40.5 ft to 41.9 ft) | TS 3.1.2.6 |
| RWST Temp | T0650A | &ge;35 F | TS 3.1.2.6 |
| RWST Conc | Chemistry | &ge;2300 and &le;2500 ppm | TS 3.1.2.6 |
| 21/22 BAST Vol | 2LI106, 2LI102 | IAW TS 3.1.2.6.a/.b and Figure 3.1-2 | TS 3.1.2.6 |
| 21/22 BAST Temp | 2TI107, 2TI103 | &ge;63 F | TS 3.1.2.6 |
| 21/22 BAST Conc | Chemistry | IAW TS 3.1.2.6.a/.b and Figure 3.1-2 | TS 3.1.2.6 |

### Notes

1. If one BAST is the borated water source, then level and concentration requirements must be maintained in the acceptable operation region of Figure 3.1-2. If two BASTs are the borated water source, then combined volumes are used to satisfy TS 3.1.2.6.a.
2. For tank capacity data, refer to S2.OP-TM.ZZ-0002(Q).

### Acceptance Criteria (Step 5.3)

- Step 5.3.1: Surveillance is considered satisfactory when Attachments 2 and 3 (as applicable) are complete with components listed meeting Acceptance Criteria stated in each Attachment.
