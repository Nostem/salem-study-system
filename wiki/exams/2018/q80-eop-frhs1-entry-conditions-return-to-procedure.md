<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; The crew is trying to establish flow from the Condensate System to feed 12 SG in accordance with 1-EOP-FRHS-1 (Response To Loss Of Secondary Heat Sink)<br>
&bull; ALL SGs NR levels are off scale low<br>
&bull; 12 SG Pressure is 550 psig and slowly lowering<br>
&bull; Containment Pressure is 6 psig and slowly rising<br>
&bull; Bleed and Feed has NOT been initiated<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
1-EOP-FRHS-1 was REQUIRED to be performed because ALL SG NR Levels are less than 15% <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 1-EOP-FRHS-1, the crew can Return To Procedure in Effect <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW EOP-CFST-1, 1-EOP-FRHS-1 was REQUIRED to be performed because ALL SG NR Levels are less than 15% and Total Feedwater Flow to SGs are less than 22E04 LB/HR. For Part 2 and IAW 1-EOP-FRHS-1 Step 19, since Bleed and Feed has NOT been initiated and the condensate line has just been aligned to feed 12 SG, the crew will return to procedure when 12 SG WR level starts to rise.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, 1-EOP-FRHS-1 Step 19 also uses ANY SG NR is &gt; than a MINIMUM of 15% (adverse) as a criteria to return to procedure and step in effect. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew will Return To Procedure in Effect ONLY after 12 SG NR is &gt; than a MINIMUM of 15%.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> For Part 1, the distractor is partially correct. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 1-EOP-FRHS-1 was REQUIRED to be performed because ALL SG NR Levels are less than 15% ONLY. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> For Part 1, the distractor is partially correct. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 1-EOP-FRHS-1 was REQUIRED to be performed because ALL SG NR Levels are less than 15% ONLY. For Part 2, 1-EOP-FRHS-1 Step 19 also uses ANY SG NR is &gt; than a MINIMUM of 15% (adverse) as a criteria to return to procedure and step in effect. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew will Return To Procedure in Effect ONLY after 12 SG NR is &gt; than a MINIMUM of 15%.</div>
