<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; The crew is performing the SG Level Control Steps of 2-EOP-FRCC-2 (Response To Degraded Core Cooling)<br>
&bull; The PO observes the following on the AUX FEEDWATER STORAGE TANK BEZEL (Red Box = VALID ALARM):<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-FRCC-2, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
The crew <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> required to shift AFW suction to an alternate source.<br>
<br>
If NO INTACT SG is available, the crew can feed a RUPTURED SG <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> is <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> or a FAULTED SG</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> is NOT <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> is NOT <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> or a FAULTED SG</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, IAW 2-EOP-FRCC-2 Step 12, since only the AFWST LO LEVEL is illuminated, the crew is NOT required to shift AFW suction to an alternate source (need the AFWST LO-LO Level Alarm to switch to the alternate suction source). For Part 2, IAW 2-EOP-FRCC-2 Step 12, If NO INTACT SG is available, the crew can feed a FAULTED SG or RUPTURED SG.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, since the AFWST Lo Level alarm is valid, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew is required to shift AFW suction to an alternate source. For Part 2, 2-EOP-FRHS-1 (RESPONSE TO LOSS OF SECONDARY HEAT SINK) specifically forbids feeding a FAULTED SG if an INTACT or RUPTURED SG is available. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 2-EOP-FRCC-2 also forbids feeding a FAULTED SG if an INTACT or RUPTURED SG is available.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, since the AFWST Lo Level alarm is valid, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew is required to shift AFW suction to an alternate source. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, 2-EOP-FRHS-1 (RESPONSE TO LOSS OF SECONDARY HEAT SINK) specifically forbids feeding a FAULTED SG if an INTACT or RUPTURED SG is available. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 2-EOP-FRCC-2 also forbids feeding a FAULTED SG if an INTACT or RUPTURED SG is available.</div>
