&bull; A Reactor Trip and Safety Injection have occurred due to a PZR Vapor Space LOCA<br>
&bull; There are indications that a bubble has formed in the Reactor Vessel<br>
&bull; The crew has just transitioned to 1-EOP-FRCI-3 (Response To Void In Reactor Vessel) from 1-EOP-LOCA-1 (Loss Of Reactor Coolant)<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
During a PZR Vapor Space LOCA, PZR level <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> a valid indication of RCS inventory.<br>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and per the WOG Transient and Accident Analysis documentation, during a PZR Vapor Space LOCA, PZR level is NOT a valid indication of RCS inventory. For Part 2 and IAW 1-EOP-FRCI-3 Step 2, since BOTH SI Pumps are running (Safety Injection has occurred), then the crew will immediately return to procedure in effect (1-EOP-LOCA-1).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, the WOG Transient and Accident Analysis documentation states that during all other LOCAs, PZR level is a valid indication of RCS inventory. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that during a PZR Vapor Space LOCA, PZR level is also a valid indication of RCS inventory. For Part 2, establishing normal flow is the first step of 1-EOP-FRCI-3 in which components are realigned (Step 3). Consequently, if the candidate fails to recognize the transition out of 1-EOP-FRCI-3 at Step 2, the candidate would incorrectly conclude that establishing normal charging is the proper action given the plant conditions.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, the WOG Transient and Accident Analysis documentation states that during all other LOCAs, PZR level is a valid indication of RCS inventory. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that during a PZR Vapor Space LOCA, PZR level is also a valid indication of RCS inventory. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, establishing normal flow is the first step of 1-EOP-FRCI-3 in which components are realigned (Step 3). Consequently, if the candidate fails to recognize the transition out of 1-EOP-FRCI-3 at Step 2, the candidate would incorrectly conclude that establishing normal charging is the proper action given the plant conditions.</div>
