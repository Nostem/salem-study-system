<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; A Unit 1 Core reload is in progress<br>
&bull; The Fuel Transfer Cart is in the Fuel Handling Building with the upender in the horizontal position<br><br>
At 09:00:00<br>
&bull; The limit switch on the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve fails and the valve no longer indicates open<br>
&bull; An NEO has verified that the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve is open<br><br>
At 09:05:00<br>
&bull; Water level in the refueling canal begins to rapidly lower<br>
&bull; The crew is preparing to close the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
At 09:00:00 and if desired, the Fuel Transfer Cart can be moved MANUALLY <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> to Containment with the failed limit switch on the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve.<br><br>
At 09:05:00, the crew <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> FULLY close the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve with the Fuel Transfer Cart located in the Fuel Handling Building.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, the Fuel Transfer Cart has two interlocks that will prevent/allow cart movement: upender must be in the horizontal position and the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve must be open (as indicated with the open limit switch). At 09:00:00, both interlocks are not satisfied and the Fuel Transfer Cart can not be moved to containment. For Part 2, the crew can not close the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve with the Fuel Transfer Cart located in the Fuel Handling Building.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve can be closed regardless of the fuel transfer cart location (inside containment or inside the fuel handling building).</div>

Part 2 is correct.</div>
