<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 95% Reactor Power and stable<br>
&bull; Condensate Polishing is in service<br>
&bull; The crew is implementing S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 (Main Feedwater / Condensate System Abnormality) IMMEDIATE ACTIONS<br>
<br>
At time 12:00<br>
&bull; 21 Header Drain Pump has tripped<br>
&bull; SGFP suction pressure is 310 psig and lowering<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
During performance of S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 IMMEDIATE ACTIONS, HU-AA-101 (Human Performance Tools And Verification Practices) states that Peer Checks are <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> (during transients).<br>
<br>
With SGFP suction pressure at 310 psig and lowering, S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 directs the crew to FIRST open <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> in an effort to restore SGFP suction pressure.

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW HU-AA-101 Step 4.8.6, "Peer checks for actions necessary to mitigate a transient are desired but not required". For Part 2 and IAW S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 Steps 3.20 through 3.22, the crew will FIRST open 21/22/23CN108 (Bypass Polisher Valves) in an effort to restore SGFP suction pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that Peer Checks are required for all IMMEDIATE ACTIONS. For Part 2, opening 2CN47 will be opened after 21/22/23CN108s are opened to restore SGFP suction pressure. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 requires 2CN47 to be opened first.</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, opening 2CN47 will be opened after 21/22/23CN108s are opened to restore SGFP suction pressure. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 requires 2CN47 to be opened first.</div>
