<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• The crew is performing 2-EOP-LOCA-5 (Loss Of Emergency Recirculation)<br>
• Containment Pressure is 12.0 psig and slowly rising<br><br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-LOCA-5, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The crew will add makeup to the RWST per <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.<br><br>
After the crew has initiated an RWST makeup, the crew will maintain TOTAL feed flow greater than 22E04 LB/HR until at least one SG NR Level is greater than <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> %.<br><br>
NOTE:<br>
• S2.OP-SO.CVC-0006 (Boron Concentration Control) Section 5.8, "Makeup to RWST Using CVCS Makeup System"<br>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW 2-EOP-LOCA-5, the crew will add makeup to the RWST per S2.OP-SO.CVC-0006 Section 5.8. For Part 2, Since Containment Pressure is 12 psig, containment is ADVERSE. Therefore IAW 2-EOP-LOCA-5, after the crew has initiated an RWST makeup, the crew will maintain TOTAL feed flow greater than 22E04 LB/HR until at least one SG NR Level is greater than 15 %.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, if containment was normal, IAW 2-EOP-LOCA-5, after the crew has initiated an RWST makeup, then the crew will maintain TOTAL feed flow greater than 22E04 LB/HR until at least one SG NR Level is greater than 9 %. Consequently, given the plant conditions, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew will maintain TOTAL feed flow greater than 22E04 LB/HR until at least one SG NR Level is greater than 9 %.</div>

Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 2-EOP-LOCA-5 directs the crew to initiate an RWST makeup per S2.OP-SO.SF-0001 Section 5.6. Part 2 is correct.</div>
