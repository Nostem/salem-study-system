&bull; Core Burnup is 8000 EFPH<br>
&bull; RCS Boron Concentration is 900 ppm<br>
&bull; Control Bank D is at 228 steps<br><br>
At time 10:00:00<br>
&bull; The crew is planning to reduce Reactor Power to 80% to support unplanned maintenance activities<br>
&bull; As part of the down power, the crew will change core reactivity by (-) 216 pcm by raising RCS Boron Concentration<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
Using the given references and disregarding any core reactivity change due to Xenon, Control Bank D will be at approximately _____ steps after the down power and RCS Boron Concentration change are completed.

Correct.</strong> IAW S2-RE-RA.ZZ-0016 (Figure 17A or Table 2-1), for the given Core Burnup and RCS Boron Concentration, lowering power from 100% to 80% will add (+) 396 pcm of reactivity to the core. Since the crew will change core reactivity by (-) 216 pcm by raising RCS Boron Concentration, Control Bank D will need to make up for the remaining (-) 180 pcm by inserting from 228 steps. Using the Integral Rod Worth from IAW S2-RE-RA.ZZ-0016 Figure 2C, Control Bank D will be at approximately 186 steps after the down power and RCS Boron Concentration change are completed.</div>
