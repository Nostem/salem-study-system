<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 25% Reactor Power and stable<br>
&bull; Emergent maintenance activities are ongoing inside containment<br>
&bull; Containment pressure is slowly rising due to the maintenance activities<br><br>
In accordance with Unit 2 Technical Specifications, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
LCO 3.6.1.4 (CONTAINMENT INTERNAL PRESSURE) will NOT be met when containment pressure is greater than a MINIMUM of + <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> psig.<br><br>
When LCO 3.6.1.4 is NOT met, the crew is required to restore containment internal pressure within limits within a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> minutes or place the Unit in HOT STANDBY within the next 6 hours.

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, LCO 3.6.1.4 (INTERNAL PRESSURE) will NOT be met when containment pressure is greater than a minimum of +0.3 psig. For Part 2, when LCO 3.6.1.4 is NOT met, the crew is required to restore containment internal pressure within limits within a MAXIMUM of 60 minutes or place the Unit in HOT STANDBY within the next 6 hours.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, the BEZEL Hi CNTMT Pressure Alarm is 0.2 psid. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly determine that LCO 3.6.1.4 will NOT be met when containment pressure is greater than a minimum of +0.2 psig. For Part 2, there are other LCOs with 30 minutes REQUIRED ACTION (LCOs 3.2.1, 3.2.4, 3.4.10.2 and 3.7.2). Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the restoration time is 30 minutes.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, the BEZEL Hi CNTMT Pressure Alarm is 0.2 psid. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, there are other LCOs with 30 minutes REQUIRED ACTION (LCOs 3.2.1, 3.2.4, 3.4.10.2 and 3.7.2). Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the restoration time is 30 minutes.</div>
