<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; The crew is performing S2.OP-AB.SF-0001 (Loss Of Spent Fuel Pool Cooling)<br>
&bull; Spent Fuel Pool temperature is 212 &deg;F<br>
&bull; Spent Fuel Pool level is 128 feet, 0 inches<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.SF-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The crew will makeup to the Spent Fuel Pool until the Spent Fuel Pool <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.<br>
The crew can perform S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 (Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill) <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> to raise Spent Fuel Pool Level.<br><br>
NOTE:<br>
&bull; S2.OP-SO.SF-0001 (Fill and Transfer of the Spent Fuel Pool)
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> (1) HI LEVEL ALARM actuates &nbsp;&nbsp; (2) ONLY</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> (1) LOW LEVEL ALARM clears &nbsp;&nbsp; (2) ONLY</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW S2.OP-AB.SF-0001, since SFP water is at the boiling point and SFP level at the Low Level alarm setpoint (128 feet, 0 inches), the crew is required to initiate makeup to the Spent Fuel Pool until the HI LEVEL ALARM actuates. For Part 2 and IAW S2.OP-AB.SF-0001, the crew can perform S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 (Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill) or S2.OP-SO.SF-0001 to raise Spent Fuel Pool Level.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, since the SFP is boiling, the candidate could conclude that any makeup must be done as fast as possible. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.SF-0001 only allows a SFP makeup using S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 (Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, since SFP Level is above the low level alarm setpoint (128 feet, 2 inches), the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.SF-0001 does not require a SFP makeup. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, since SFP Level is above the low level alarm setpoint (128 feet, 2 inches), the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.SF-0001 does not require a SFP makeup. For Part 2, since the SFP is boiling, the candidate could conclude that any makeup must be done as fast as possible. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.SF-0001 only allows a SFP makeup using S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 (Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill).</div>
