<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; The crew is performing 2-EOP-FRCE-2 (Response To High Containment Sump Level)<br>
&bull; SW flow to 25 CFCU is abnormally high<br>
&bull; 25 CFCU has just been stopped<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-CFST-1 (Critical Safety Function Status Trees), the crew entered 2-EOP-FRCE-2 when Containment Sump Level reached a minimum of <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> %.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-FRCE-2, the crew will close 25SW58 (Inlet Water Valve) <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, IAW 2-EOP-CFST-1, the crew entered 2-EOP-FRCE-2 when Containment Sump Level reached a minimum of 78% (75% adverse). Since the question stem indicates containment pressure is normal, the crew entered 2-EOP-FRCE-2 when Containment Sump Level reached a minimum of 78%. For Part 2, in accordance with 2-EOP-FRCE-2 Step 1, the crew will close 25SW58 (Inlet Water Valve) and 25SW72 (Outlet Water Valve).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, Containment Sump Level of 75% is the adverse level in which EOP-CFST-1 requires performance of EOP-FRCE-2. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude for the given plant conditions that 2-EOP-FRCE-2 is entered when Containment Sump Level reached a minimum of 75%. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, Containment Sump Level of 75% is the adverse level in which EOP-CFST-1 requires performance of EOP-FRCE-2. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude for the given plant conditions that 2-EOP-FRCE-2 is entered when Containment Sump Level reached a minimum of 75%. For Part 2, when 25 CFCU was stopped, 25SW223 automatically closed. 25SW223 is directly downstream of 25SW72. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 2-EOP-FRCE-2 only requires the affected CFCU's SW Inlet valve (SW58) to be closed.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, when 25 CFCU was stopped, 25SW223 automatically closed. 25SW223 is directly downstream of 25SW72. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that 2-EOP-FRCE-2 only requires the affected CFCU's SW Inlet valve (SW58) to be closed.</div>
