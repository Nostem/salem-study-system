<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; 2R44A and 2R44B (High Range Containment Accident Monitors) are in alarm<br>
&bull; Containment Radiation is 7E2 R/HR<br>
&bull; The crew has just entered 2-EOP-FRCE-3 (Response To High Containment Radiation)<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-CFST-1 and based on the Containment Radiation levels, Containment is considered <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-FRCE-3, the crew is required to operate the 21 THRU 25 CFCUs in <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> speed.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> NORMAL <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> low</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> NORMAL <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> high</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> ADVERSE <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> low</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> ADVERSE <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> high</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, IAW 2-EOP-CFST-1, since containment radiation is less than 1E05 R/HR, containment is considered NORMAL. For Part 2, IAW 2-EOP-FRCE-3 Step 2, the crew is required to operate the 21 THRU 25 CFCUs in low speed.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that running the CFCUs in high speed would better circulate the containment atmosphere which would better mitigate the high radiation condition.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> For Part 1, since 2R44A and 2R44B (High Range Containment Accident Monitors) are in alarm, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that containment is ADVERSE. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> For Part 1, since 2R44A and 2R44B (High Range Containment Accident Monitors) are in alarm, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that containment is ADVERSE. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that running the CFCUs in high speed would better circulate the containment atmosphere which would better mitigate the high radiation condition.</div>
