<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q14 — Containment Spray Pump Power Supplies</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">026 CSS-K2.01 (3.4)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- A LOCA coincident with a Loss Of Offsite Power has occurred at Unit 2<br>
- The 2C 4KV Vital Bus is de-energized due to a fault on the bus<br><br>
At time 16:30:00<br>
- Containment Pressure is 15.1 psig and slowly rising<br><br>
At 16:30:30, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
___ Containment Spray Pump(s) is/are running
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> NO</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> BOTH</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> ONLY 21</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> ONLY 22</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> 21 CS Pump is powered from 2A 4KV Vital Bus and 22 CS Pump is powered from 2C 4KV Vital Bus. Additionally, a Containment Spray actuation occurs at 15 psig Containment pressure. At 16:30:00, a containment spray actuation signal exists and 2C 4KV Vital Bus is de-energized. Consequently, ONLY 21 CS Pump is running.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> The candidate could incorrectly conclude that at 16:30:00, the Containment Spray setpoint has not been reached or that the electrical transient has affected both CS pumps. In either case, the candidate would then incorrectly deduce that no CS pumps are running.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> The candidate could incorrectly conclude that both CS pumps are not powered from the 2C 4KV Vital Bus. Consequently, with a valid Containment Spray signal present, the candidate would incorrectly deduce that both CS pumps are running.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> The candidate could incorrectly conclude that only the 21 CS Pump is powered from 2C 4KV Vital Bus. Consequently, with a valid Containment Spray signal present, the candidate would incorrectly deduce that only 22 CS pump is running.</div>
