<strong>Given:</strong><br>
<br>
At time 07:00<br>
&bull; A Reactor Trip and Safety Injection have occurred due to a LBLOCA<br>
<br>
At time 07:20<br>
&bull; The crew has transitioned to 1-EOP-LOCA-1 (Loss Of Reactor Coolant)<br>
<br>
In accordance with 1-EOP-LOCA-1 (Loss Of Reactor Coolant), which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
The crew will start <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> Hydrogen Recombiner(s) when Containment Hydrogen Concentration is greater than 0.5%.<br>
<br>
<span class="blank">_(2)_</span> is the EARLIEST time that the crew is required to perform 1-EOP-LOCA-4 (Transfer To Hot Leg Recirculation).
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW 1-EOP-LOCA-1, the crew will start ONLY one Hydrogen Recombiner when Containment Hydrogen Concentration is greater than 0.5%. For Part 2 and IAW 1-EOP-LOCA-1, the crew is required to perform 1-EOP-LOCA-4 14 hours after the SI Actuation. Since the SI Actuation occurred at 07:00, the crew is required to perform 1-EOP-LOCA-4 at 21:00.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, EOP-LOCA-1 has a requirement to start preparations for Hot Leg Recirculation 12 hours after the SI Actuation. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew is required to perform 1-EOP-LOCA-4 at 19:00.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> For Part 1, there are two hydrogen recombiners. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that both hydrogen recombiners will be started during the performance of EOP-LOCA-1. For Part 2, EOP-LOCA-1 has a requirement to start preparations for Hot Leg Recirculation 12 hours after the SI Actuation. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the crew is required to perform 1-EOP-LOCA-4 at 19:00.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> For Part 1, there are two hydrogen recombiners. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that both hydrogen recombiners will be started during the performance of EOP-LOCA-1. Part 2 is correct.</div>
