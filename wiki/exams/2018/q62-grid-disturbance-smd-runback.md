<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q62 — Grid Disturbance SMD Runback Controls</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE: 077 Generator Voltage and Electric Grid Disturbances-AA1.02 (3.8)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 100% Reactor Power<br>
&bull; The crew is performing S2.OP-AB.GRID-0001 (Abnormal Grid) Attachment 2 (Solar Magnetic Disturbance)<br>
&bull; The crew is preparing to initiate a generator load reduction to less than 942 MW (~77.6% RTP) at 15% / MIN from the TURBINE E-H CONTROL & STATUS touch screen monitor<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.GRID-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
With the turbine controls in OPERATOR MODE - AUTO, the PO will select ___ to initiate the generator load reduction.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> SMD#1 RUNBACK ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> SMD#2 RUNBACK ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> SMD#1 RUNBACK then GO</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> SMD#2 RUNBACK then GO</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.GRID-0001 (Abnormal Grid) Attachment 2 (Solar Magnetic Disturbance), the PO will depress SMD#1 RUNBACK and GO to initiate the generator load reduction.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. The TURBINE E-H CONTROL & STATUS touch screen monitor allows for manual entry of the setter and the reduction ramp rate. During manual entry the turbine won't move until the go button is depressed. Since the SMD#1 RUNBACK is a preset setter position with a preset ramp rate, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that only the SMD#1 RUNBACK button is depressed to start the load reduction.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. The TURBINE E-H CONTROL & STATUS touch screen monitor allows for manual entry of the setter and the reduction ramp rate. During manual entry the turbine won't move until the go button is depressed. Additionally, since there is a SMD#2 RUNBACK with a preset setter position with a preset ramp rate, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that only the SMD#2 RUNBACK button is depressed to start the load reduction.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. The candidate could incorrectly conclude that using the SMD#2 RUNBACK is required per S2.OP-AB.GRID-0001 Attachment 2. Pressing GO is required to start the turbine load reduction.</div>
