<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, with the plant initially at 100% Power, the Steam Dump System is in the T<sub>AVG</sub> Mode. Consequently when the plant trips and ONLY Reactor Trip Train A Breaker opens, the Load Rejection Controller will control the steam dumps. If Reactor Trip Train B Breaker had opened then the Plant Trip Controller would have controlled the steam dumps. For Part 2, with the Load Rejection Controller in control of the steam dumps, there is a 4&deg;F dead band associated with the controller. Consequently, the steam dumps will maintain RCS T<sub>AVG</sub> at 551&deg;F (547&deg;F (no load T<sub>AVG</sub>) + 5&deg;F).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that steam dump Plant Trip Controller is based on Reactor Trip Train A Breaker opening. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly recall which steam dump controller has a dead band and incorrectly conclude that the Plant Trip Controller has a dead band, resulting in 551&deg;F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the Plant Trip Controller is based on Train A Breaker opening. For Part 2, the Plant Trip Controller has no dead band and will normally restore RCS T<sub>AVG</sub> to RCS No-Load T<sub>AVG</sub> (547&deg;F).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, following a reactor trip in which both Reactor Trip Train A and B Breakers open, the steam dump system would maintain RCS T<sub>AVG</sub> at 547&deg;F. The candidate could incorrectly recall which controller has a dead band.</div>
