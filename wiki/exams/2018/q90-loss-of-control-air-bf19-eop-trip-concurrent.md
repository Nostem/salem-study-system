• The crew enters S2.OP-AB.CA-0001 (Loss Of Control Air)<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.CA-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, IAW S2.OP-AB.CA-0001, the BF19s (FW CONT VALVE) will fail closed as Control Air pressure lowers. Consequently, SG levels are expected to lower. For Part 2, IAW S2.OP-AB.CA-0001 (Selected CAS Items), once the reactor is tripped, the crew will perform 2-EOP-TRIP-1 and S2.OP-AB.CA-0001 concurrently.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the BF19s (FW CONT VALVE) will fail open as Control Air pressure lowers. Consequently, the candidate would then incorrectly deduce that the SG levels are expected to rise. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the BF19s (FW CONT VALVES) will fail open as Control Air pressure lowers. Consequently, the candidate would then incorrectly deduce that the SG levels are expected to rise. For Part 2, other Abnormal Procedures (e.g. OP-AB-COND-001) require the crew to only perform EOP-TRIP-1 when reactor trip criteria have been met. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that when reactor trip criteria have been met in S2.OP-AB.CA-0001, the crew will only perform 2-EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, other Abnormal Procedures (e.g. OP-AB-COND-001) require the crew to only perform EOP-TRIP-1 when reactor trip criteria have been met. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that when reactor trip criteria have been met in S2.OP-AB.CA-0001, the crew will only perform 2-EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
