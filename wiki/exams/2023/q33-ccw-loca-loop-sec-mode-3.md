<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 LOCA concurrent with LOOP<br>
• Crew is in EOP-TRIP-1<br><br>
What is the status of the CCW system?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> CCW pumps remain running, both HXs available.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> No CCW pumps running; SW to CCW HXs auto-isolated to prevent SW pump runout.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> No CCW pumps running; SW to CCW HXs must be manually isolated.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> All CCW pumps running; SW to CCW HXs isolated.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> SEC Mode 3 (SI + Blackout): all CCW pumps are tripped. SW to CCW HXs is auto-isolated to prevent SW pump runout with only 3 SW pumps available on emergency power. CCW pumps are not sequenced onto the EDGs due to loading concerns. CCW pumps are started later per EOP-APPX-1 when needed for cold leg recirculation.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. This describes SEC Mode 1 (SI only, no blackout). In a concurrent LOCA + LOOP (SEC Mode 3), CCW pumps are tripped and not sequenced onto EDGs.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. No CCW pumps running is correct, but SW to CCW HXs is auto-isolated in SEC Mode 3, not manually isolated. The auto-isolation prevents SW pump runout.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect. This describes SEC Mode 2 (blackout only, no SI). In a concurrent LOCA + LOOP (SEC Mode 3), CCW pumps are tripped, not running.</div>
