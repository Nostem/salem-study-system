<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 lost all feedwater, performing EOP-FRHS-1<br>
• SI manually actuated and reset<br>
• 24 SG selected for depressurization<br>
• MSLI on all SGs except 24<br>
• 22MS167 failed to close<br>
• Depressurization rate set too HIGH<br><br>
What happens and how does the crew continue depressurization?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> No effect, MSLI signals blocked when SI reset</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Hi steam line diff pressure causes MSLI, use atmospheric dumps</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Hi steam flow with Lo-Lo Tavg causes MSLI, use atmospheric dumps</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Lo-Lo Tavg (P-12) closes steam dumps, bypass Tavg pushbuttons to continue</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> 100% steam dump causes high steam flows and Lo-Lo Tavg (P-12). P-12 closes steam dumps AND high steam flow with P-12 actuates MSLI closing all MSIVs. No steam dumps available. Continue depressurization via MS10s (atmospheric dumps). MSLI signals for high steam flow CANNOT be blocked.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Manually actuating and resetting SI does NOT block MSLI signals. MSLI for high steam flow cannot be blocked.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> High steamline differential pressure causes SI, not MSLI. The correct MSLI signal is high steam flow coincident with Lo-Lo Tavg.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> P-12 does close steam dumps, but MSLI also actuates on high steam flow with Lo-Lo Tavg. MSLI signals cannot be blocked or bypassed with pushbuttons.</div>
