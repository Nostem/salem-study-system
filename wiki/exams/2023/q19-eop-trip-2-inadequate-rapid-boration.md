<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q19 — EOP-TRIP-2 / Inadequate Rapid Boration Flow</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE 24 AA2.12 (3.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 reactor trip, crew in EOP-TRIP-2<br>
• 3 control rods not fully inserted<br>
• Rapid boration via BAT pump<br>
• 2CV175 fully open<br>
• Flowmeter reads 20 gpm<br><br>
What is the required action?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Continue rapid boration until required boron achieved</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Stop BAT pump, realign charging suction to RWST, maintain charging >87 gpm</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Establish rapid boration via cold leg injection from RWST and BIT valves</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Locally open 2CV174 to bypass blender, open 2CV172 for max boron, maintain charging >87 gpm</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> Per EOP-TRIP-2 step 4, adequate rapid boration flow through 2CV175 is 36 gpm. Indicated 20 gpm is inadequate. EOP-TRIP-2 directs: stop BAT pump, realign charging suction from RWST, and maintain charging flow greater than 87 gpm.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Rapid boration flow should be 36 gpm; 20 gpm is inadequate. Continuing with inadequate flow does not meet the procedure requirements.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> This action is from EOP-FRSM-1 (subcriticality response), not EOP-TRIP-2.</div>
