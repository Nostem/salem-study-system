<div style="margin-top:12px;padding:10px;background:#1a1400;border-left:3px solid #fbbf24;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;">
<strong style="color:#fbbf24;">Work Hour Limits (Non-Outage, LS-AA-119-1003):</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">
- Cannot exceed 16 hours in any continuous 24-hour period<br>
- Cannot exceed 26 hours in any continuous 48-hour period<br>
- Cannot exceed 72 hours in any continuous 168-hour period<br>
- 34-hour continuous break required in any 9-day period<br>
- 10-hour continuous break minimum between shifts
</span>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:12px;padding:10px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;">

<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">Operator 1 fails the 34-hour break rule (only 33 hours continuous break in the past 9 days). Operator 3 fails the 72-hour/7-day rule (73 hours total). Only Operator 2 meets all five work hour requirements and can take the watch.</span>
