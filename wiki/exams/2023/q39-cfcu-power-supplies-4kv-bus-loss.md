<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> With 2B 4KV Vital Bus de-energized: 22 CFCU (B bus) and 24 CFCU (B bus) cannot run. With 23 CFCU CIT for maintenance: 23 cannot run. Only 21 CFCU (A bus) and 25 CFCU (C bus) remain available and running.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. 22 CFCU is powered from the B bus, which is de-energized. 22 cannot run. 25 (C bus) is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. Both 22 and 24 CFCUs are powered from the B bus, which is de-energized. Neither can run.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. 24 CFCU is powered from the B bus, which is de-energized. 24 cannot run. 21 (A bus) is correct.</div>
