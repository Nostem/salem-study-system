<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> The 2FP147 requires to be manually opened at the 2RP5 panel when a valid fire is detected inside containment. The 2FP147 will close on a Phase A signal.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because fire protection systems will actuate if the fire detector sensor is triggered. Incorrect in that even though the fire zone fire water header is triggered, the 2FP147 still needs to be manually opened from the control room.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because fire protection systems will actuate if the fire detector sensor is triggered. Incorrect in that even though the fire zone fire water header is triggered, the 2FP147 still needs to be manually opened from the control room.</div>
