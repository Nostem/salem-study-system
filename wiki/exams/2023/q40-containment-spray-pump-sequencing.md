<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">026 K2.01 (3.9)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Salem Unit 2 experienced a LOCA<br>
• Containment pressure is 10 psig and slowly rising<br>
• ALL available safeguards equipment have started with the exception that 2B SEC failed to actuate<br>
• SECs have NOT been RESET<br><br>
Based on the conditions above, which of the following completes the statement?<br><br>
_(1)_ containment spray pump(s) is/are still available from a Class 1E vital power source and _(2)_ automatically start when containment pressure exceeds the containment pressure HI-HI setpoint.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Only one CS pump available; WILL auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Only one CS pump available; will NOT auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> BOTH CS pumps available; WILL auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> BOTH CS pumps available; will NOT auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
</div>

<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> Both CS pumps are powered from A and C 4KV vital buses. The 2B SEC failure does not affect CS pumps since neither is powered from the B bus. A and C SECs actuated in Mode 1. With the sequence complete and SECs NOT reset, the CS pump start contact is re-closed — CS pumps WILL auto-start when the Hi-Hi containment pressure signal is received.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. Both CS pumps are available, not just one. 21 CS pump is on the A bus (A SEC) and 22 CS pump is on the C bus (C SEC). The failed B SEC does not power either CS pump.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. Both CS pumps are available (not just one), and they will auto-start. With SECs not reset, the CS start contact remains active for auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect. Both CS pumps being available is correct, but they WILL auto-start. Since SECs have NOT been reset, the CS pump start contact remains re-closed after the initial sequence, allowing auto-start on Hi-Hi containment pressure.</div>
