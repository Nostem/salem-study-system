# 2023 Q78 — RCP Vibration Abnormal Procedure Actions

<div style="background:#111827;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:8px;padding:20px;margin-bottom:24px;">
<div style="display:flex;justify-content:space-between;align-items:center;margin-bottom:12px;">
<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q78 — RCP Vibration Abnormal Procedure Actions</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">T1G1 APE 15 AA2.16 (3.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- Salem Unit 2 is operating at 30% power<br>
- OHA D-36, RCP VIB HI, is LIT<br>
- 21 RCP parameters are:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Motor flange vibration is 3 mils and steady<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Shaft vibration is 16 mils and steady<br>
- Engineering has determined the vibrations are valid<br>
- The crew enters S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, RCP Abnormality<br><br>
What action will the CRS direct?<br><br>
Note: TS 3.4.1.1, Reactor Coolant Loops and Coolant Circulation<br>
2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip or Safety Injection
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Trip the reactor and stop 21 RCP per Attachment 2, Stopping RCPs, of S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Preparations for a controlled Unit shutdown to remove 21 RCP from service.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Stop 21 RCP within 1 hour per TS 3.4.1.1 and place the Unit in MODE 3 within 6 hours.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Trip the reactor and stop 21 RCP per OHA D-36 Alarm Response Procedure and enter 2-EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> The RCP vibration levels are below the requirements in Attachment 1 of AB.RCP-1 that require stopping the affected RCP per Attachment 2. The OHA ARP for D-36 directs entry into S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001. Per S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 Step 3.10 allows the CRS/SM to determine if a Unit shutdown is required.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because motor and flange vibrations are parameters on Attachment 1 of S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 that may require stopping RCPs per Attachment 2. Incorrect in that the Attachment 1 RCP vibration levels have not been exceeded as of yet.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible if the applicant believes that since RCP trip criteria has not been reached per Attachment 1 of S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, then take the action of TS 3.4.1.1 for a loss of an RCP. Incorrect in that the TS action is to place the unit in MODE 3 within one hour on a loss of RCS flow.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible if the applicant believes that the ARP will direct stopping and tripping the reactor which it does not, only directs entry into S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001.</div>
