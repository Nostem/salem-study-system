<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">T2G2 002 G2.2.37 (4.6)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- Salem Unit 2 is at 100% power<br>
- 21 Charging Pump is in service<br>
- Surveillance test S2.OP-ST.RC-0007, Seal Injection Flow, was just completed.<br>
<br>
The following test data was recorded:<br>
- Charging Header pressure = 2450 psig<br>
- 2CV55 fully open<br>
- 21 RCP seal injection flow = 10 gpm<br>
- 22 RCP seal injection flow = 10.5 gpm<br>
- 23 RCP seal injection flow = 9.8 gpm<br>
- 24 RCP seal injection flow = 10.2 gpm<br>
<br>
Based on the surveillance test data, what action is required?<br>
<br>
[REFERENCE PROVIDED]
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Restore the inoperable Charging Pump to Operable status within 72 hours per TS 3.5.2 ECCS Subsystems Tavg > 350 °F.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> No action is required, the surveillance test is satisfactory.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Adjust manual seal injection throttle valves to within the limit within 4 hours as required by TS 3.5.4, Seal Injection Flow.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> No TS action is required unless the seal injection flows can NOT be adjusted to within the limits within 4 hours.</div>
</div>
