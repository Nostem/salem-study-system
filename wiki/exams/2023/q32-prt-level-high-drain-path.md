<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Salem Unit 2 is at 100% power<br>
• Console Alarm PRT LEVEL HI-LO is LIT<br>
• PRT level is 90%<br><br>
Which of the following completes the statement?<br><br>
PRT level will be lowered ...
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Via RCDT Pump to CVCS Hold-Up Tank</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Gravity drain to CVCS Hold-Up Tank</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Gravity drain to Aux Building Sump</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> Per S2.OP-SO.PZR-0003: open 2PR14 to drain PRT to RCDT, which automatically opens 2WL12 and starts the RCDT pump (defeats low level cutoff). RCDT pumps discharge to: RWST, CVCS Hold-Up Tanks, or Waste Hold-Up Tanks.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. The PRT does drain via the RCDT pump, but the RCDT pump discharges to the RWST, CVCS Hold-Up Tanks, or Waste Hold-Up Tanks — not to the Aux Building Sump.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. The PRT does not gravity drain to the CVCS Hold-Up Tank. The drain path requires the RCDT pump to transfer the PRT contents.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect. The PRT does not gravity drain, and the destination is not the Aux Building Sump. The RCDT pump is required, and it discharges to RWST, CVCS HUTs, or WHUTs.</div>
