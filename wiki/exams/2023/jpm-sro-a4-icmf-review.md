- 33 Ft. level wind direction is from 180 degrees at 5 mph<br>
- No additional operator actions have been taken<br><br>
<strong>Initiating Cue:</strong><br>
The Shift Manager directs you to perform an accuracy peer check on the provided Initial Contact Message Form (ICMF).<br>
Document any errors on the attached worksheet, should any exist.<br>
