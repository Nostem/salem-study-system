<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q77 — LOCA Containment Spray vs FRP Purple Path Priority</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">T1G1 EPE 11 G2.4.14 (4.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- A LOCA is in progress on Salem Unit 2.<br>
- The crew is implementing 2-EOP-LOCA-1, Loss of Reactor or Secondary Coolant, and is currently at Step 7.4 for assessing conditions for stopping containment spray pumps.<br>
- Containment pressure is 23 psig.<br><br>
<strong>Then:</strong><br>
- A valid PURPLE path exists on Thermal Shock<br>
- Containment pressure is below 13 psig.<br><br>
In accordance with OP-SA-108-101-2002, EOP Users Guide, which of the following is required and why?<br><br>
Note: FRP = Functional Restoration Procedure(s)
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Step 7.4 may be performed if the CRS determines there is no conflict with implementing the FRP.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Immediately perform Step 7.4; a Note step in EOP-LOCA-1 specifically states NO FRPs are to be implemented during performance of EOP-LOCA-1.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Immediately perform Step 7.4; Step 7.4 is a continuous action step and still in effect.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> DO NOT perform Step 7.4; transition to FRP for PURPLE path.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> Per OP-SA-108-101-2002 step 2.16.2, a continuous action step should not be performed when a Red or Purple path FRP is in progress. Therefore, even though conditions of LOCA-1 step 7.4 are met, the crew should not stop CS pumps.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the operator may believe that if a conflict arises between actions of an EOP and FRP that the CRS will always have the option to determine the best path to take, for example "Thinking Compliance".</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because EOP-LOCA-3 has a Note (N1-1) that does state to NOT implement FRPs while performing actions to establish cold leg recirculation.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because step 7.4 is a continuous action step and would be performed when conditions are met. Incorrect in that continuous actions are not performed when transitions are made to a Red or Purple path FRP.</div>
