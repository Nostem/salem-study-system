<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Salem Unit 2 is at 100% power<br>
Then:<br>
• OHA D-20, 21 RCP BRG CLG WTR FLO LO, is Alarming<br>
• RO reports that all RCP motor bearing temperatures are rising<br><br>
Which of the following completes the statement?<br><br>
Per S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, RCP Abnormality, the crew has a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">(1)</span> minutes to restore CCW flow OR is required to trip the reactor and stop the RCP anytime RCP motor bearing temperatures exceeds <span class="blank">(2)</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 2 minutes; 175&deg;F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 2 minutes; 165&deg;F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 5 minutes; 165&deg;F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 5 minutes; 175&deg;F</div>

<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> Per AB.RCP-0001 Attachment 1: 5 minutes to restore CCW or trip reactor and stop affected RCP. Anytime motor bearing temperature exceeds 175&deg;F, trip reactor and stop affected RCP. Additional trip criteria: motor winding temp &gt;302&deg;F, seal water outlet &gt;190&deg;F, shaft vibration &gt;20 mils, #1 seal leakoff &lt;0.8 gpm or &ge;6 gpm.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. 2 minutes is the time limit for loss of seal injection AND thermal barrier CCW concurrently, not for loss of CCW to motor bearings alone. 175&deg;F is the correct motor bearing temperature limit.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. 2 minutes is the concurrent seal injection/thermal barrier CCW loss time limit, not the CCW loss time limit. 165&deg;F is the ARP limit that directs transition to AB.RCP-0001, not the AB trip limit.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. 5 minutes is the correct CCW restoration time limit, but 165&deg;F is the ARP limit directing entry into AB.RCP-0001. The AB.RCP-0001 trip limit for motor bearing temperature is 175&deg;F.</div>
