<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q69 — Emergency Plan / Primary Communicator / Notification Timing</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">T3 G2.4.29 (3.1)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- You are the Primary Communicator<br>
<br>
<strong>Then:</strong><br>
- A station event has just been classified as a Site Area Emergency<br>
<br>
Which of the following describes the time requirement for State and Local notifications?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 30 minutes from the initiation of the event.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 15 minutes from the completion of the initial contact message form.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 15 minutes from the time the communicator acknowledges the briefing from the Shift Manager.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 15 minutes from the classification of the event.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> The 15 minute notification time to the States and Local agencies starts when the Shift Manager classifies or declares the emergency classification for the event.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the operator may believe that the notification time includes the 15 minutes to classify and 15 minutes for the notification to the States. Incorrect in that the classification time can be less than 15 minutes, therefore 30 minutes for notifications is not always correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because the operator as the primary communicator usually waits until the ICMF is completed by the Shift Manager and is read to the communicator before notifying the States. Incorrect in that the notification time of 15 minutes starts when the Shift Manager declares the emergency classification.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because the operator as the primary communicator usually waits until the ICMF is completed by the Shift Manager and is read to the communicator before notifying the States. Incorrect in that the notification time of 15 minutes starts when the Shift Manager declares the emergency classification.</div>
