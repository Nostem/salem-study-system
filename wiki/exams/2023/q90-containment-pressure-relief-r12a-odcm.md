• A Containment Pressure Relief is in progress<br><br>
<strong>Then:</strong><br>
• 2R12A, Containment Atmosphere Radiation Monitor, fails due to a power supply failure<br>
• The Containment Pressure Relief is stopped<br><br>
Based on the above condition, which of the following is ONLY required per the ODCM to recommence the containment pressure relief (assume all other Radiation Monitors are OPERABLE)?<br><br>

Per the ODCM requirements, the Containment Pressure Relief ...

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> may recommence provided two independent samples of containment are analyzed and two release rate calculations are independently verified.</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Since 2R41 channels are operable (assumption in stem) then ODCM 3.3.9 is MET and no additional action is required to recommence the containment pressure relief.</div>
