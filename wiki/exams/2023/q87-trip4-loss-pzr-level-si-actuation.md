<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q87 — EOP-TRIP-4 Loss of PZR Level / SI Actuation</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">T2G1 006 A2.12 (4.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• A Loss of Offsite Power has occurred on Salem Unit 2<br>
• The crew is performing actions in 2-EOP-TRIP-4, Natural Circulation Cooldown<br>
• 21 Charging Pump is running<br>
• RCS cooldown to establish required subcooling is in progress<br>
• Low PZR Pressure SI signal is BLOCKED<br>
• RCS temperature is 537 degrees F<br><br>
<strong>Then:</strong><br>
• RCS pressure is 1183 psig and lowering<br>
• 2CV55, Charging Flow Control Valve, is fully open<br>
• 2CV71, Seal Injection Backpressure Valve, is fully open<br>
• Pressurizer level is 10% and lowering<br><br>
Which of the following describes the correct action(s) for these conditions per 2-EOP-TRIP-4?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Stop the RCS cooldown and wait until PZR level and subcooling are restored.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Align the Charging Pump suction to the RWST, open the BIT isolation valves, and transition to 2-EOP-LOCA-1, Loss of Reactor or Secondary Coolant.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Operate Pressurizer heaters as necessary to maintain saturated conditions in the pressurizer per 2-EOP-TRIP-4.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Actuate Safety Injection and return to 2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip or Safety Injection.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> Per the foldout page (CAS) of 2-EOP-TRIP-4, if subcooling is 0 degrees F or PZR level can NOT be maintained greater than 4%, then Actuate SI and go to 2-EOP-TRIP-1. In this case subcooling is greater than 0 degrees F, but PZR level is lowering and cannot be maintained based on the 2CV55 and 2CV71 fully open. Letdown is isolated.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because operator may believe that the reason for the PZR level lowering is due to excessive cooldown of the RCS.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because this is similar to CAS step 6.1 in EOP-TRIP-4. Incorrect in that TRIP-4 does not direct entry into EOP-LOCA-1.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because this is similar to CAS step 3 in EOP-TRIP-4. Incorrect in that PZR heaters will not energize with level less than 17%.</div>
