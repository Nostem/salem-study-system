<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- Salem Unit 2 is at 100% power<br>
<strong>Then:</strong><br>
- The ventilation system that supplies cool air to the room that the Reference Junction Panel is located for the In-Core Temperature Monitoring System is lost<br>
- Room temperature have risen by 15 degrees F<br>
<br>
Which of the following describes the impact on the In-Core Monitoring instrumentation and what is its design limit?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> CET readings will indicate higher than actual; up to 3300 degrees F.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> CET readings will indicate higher than actual; up to 2300 degrees F.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> NO effect on CET readings as they are temperature compensated; up to 2300 degrees F.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> NO effect on CET readings as they are temperature compensated; up to 3300 degrees F.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> The reference junction box is located outside of containment. Ambient temperature changes in the room will affect the reference junction box, however the system uses a temperature compensator to adjust for the changes in temperatures where the reference junction box is located. A rise in ambient room temperature will raise temperature, but the temperature compensating circuit will adjust for these reference junction box temperature changes. In-Core system design upper range is 2300 degrees F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because higher temperatures in the room where the reference junction box is located will cause the in temperature indicated to rise. Incorrect in that the system has a temperature compensator circuit such that the reference junction box temperature changes are adjusted to the true thermocouple reading. In-core system has a design range to 2300 degrees F.</div>
