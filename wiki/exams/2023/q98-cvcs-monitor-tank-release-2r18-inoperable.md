- A release of 21 CVCS Monitor Tank needs to be performed<br>

<br>
Which of the following completes both statements?<br>
<br>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> Per the ODCM 3.3.8 Radioactive Liquid Effluent Monitoring Instrumentation, with 2R18 inoperable action 26 applies which requires at least two independent samples are analyzed and at least two technically qualified members of the Facility Staff independently verify the release rate calculations and discharge line valving. Per S2.OP-SO.WL-0001, step 2.5 the SM/CRS authorizes the release.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Part 1 is correct. Part 2 is incorrect. Plausible because surveys of piping are performed but for different reason. Incorrect in that the ODCM requires two independent samples analyzed prior to the release which is verified by Chemistry.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is incorrect. Plausible because the SRO may confuse the responsibility of a radioactive liquid release with a Rad Pro function. Part 2 is incorrect. Plausible because surveys of piping are performed but for different reason. Incorrect in that the ODCM requires two independent samples analyzed prior to the release which is verified by Chemistry.</div>

Plausible because the SRO may confuse the responsibility of a radioactive liquid release with a Rad Pro function. Part 2 is correct.</div>
