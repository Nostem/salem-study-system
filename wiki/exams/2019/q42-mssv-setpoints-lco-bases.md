<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
In accordance with Unit 2 LCO 3.7.1.1 (TURBINE CYCLE SAFETY VALVES), which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The Main Steam Line Code Safety Valves will start to open when main steam line pressure FIRST exceeds <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> psig.<br><br>
The purpose of LCO 3.7.1.1 is to limit the secondary system pressure to within 110% of its design pressure <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> (1) 1100 (2) ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> (1) 1100 (2) and to protect against overpressurization of the Reactor Coolant Pressure boundary</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> (1) 1070 (2) ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> (1) 1070 (2) and to protect against overpressurization of the Reactor Coolant Pressure boundary</div>
</div>

<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW LCO 3.7.1.1, the Main Steam Line Code Safety Valves will start to open when main steam line pressure FIRST exceeds 1070 psig. For Part 2 and IAW LCO 3.7.1.1, The purpose of LCO 3.7.1.1 is to limit the secondary system pressure to within 110% of its design pressure and to protect against overpressurization of the Reactor Coolant Pressure boundary.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> For Part 1, MSSVs have 5 different setpoints and 1100 psig is the second lowest setpoint for 21-24MS14s. For Part 2, the candidate could not recognize that the MSSVs also protect against overpressurization of the Reactor Coolant Pressure boundary.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> For Part 1, MSSVs have 5 different setpoints and 1100 psig is the second lowest setpoint for 21-24MS14s. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could not recognize that the MSSVs also protect against overpressurization of the Reactor Coolant Pressure boundary.</div>
