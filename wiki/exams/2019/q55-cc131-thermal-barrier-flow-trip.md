<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, 2CC131 (RCP THERMAL BARRIER VALVE) AUTOMATICALLY closed when CC flow from the RCP Thermal Barrier reached a MINIMUM of 175 gpm. For Part 2 and IAW S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 CAS, with only Thermal Barrier Component Cooling flows lost (RCP Injection Flow is normal), the crew is NOT required to secure ALL RCPs within 2 minutes.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, 145 gpm is lo flow alarm setpoint of CC131. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that CC131 will automatically close when flow reaches a minimum of 145 gpm. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, 145 gpm is lo flow alarm setpoint of CC131. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that CC131 will automatically close when flow reaches a minimum of 145 gpm. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly determine that RCP seal cooling has been lost and determine that in accordance with S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 CAS, the crew is required to secure ALL RCPs within 2 minutes.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly determine that RCP seal cooling has been lost and determine that in accordance with S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 CAS, the crew is required to secure ALL RCPs within 2 minutes.</div>
