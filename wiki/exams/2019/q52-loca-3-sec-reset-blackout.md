In accordance with 2-EOP-LOCA-3, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
At 10:00 and after resetting ALL of the SECs, the crew <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> REQUIRED to ensure that the 230V control centers are reset.<br>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, at 10:00 and after resetting ALL of the SECs, the crew is required to MANUALLY reset the 230V control centers (even though at 20 minutes after SEC actuation, this lockout is automatically removed). For Part 2, if a blackout loading sequence occurs on any 4KV vital bus with SI previously reset, then ECCS pumps and other safeguards equipment will not automatically start and must be manually operated if required since different equipment is started on a SEC blackout loading than on a safeguards loading.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that after the blackout loading is completed, the ECCS pumps and safeguards equipment on 2A 4KV Vital Bus will start AUTOMATICALLY (since a LBLOCA is in progress).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate may fail to recognize that after resetting ALL of the SECs, the crew is required to MANUALLY reset the 230V control centers. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate may fail to recognize that after resetting ALL of the SECs, the crew is required to MANUALLY reset the 230V control centers. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that after the blackout loading is completed, the ECCS pumps and safeguards equipment on 2A 4KV Vital Bus will start AUTOMATICALLY (since a LBLOCA is in progress).</div>
