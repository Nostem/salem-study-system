<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below concerning the 2A EDG?<br>
<br>
The 2A EDG can be run at a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> KW for 2000 hours.<br>
<br>
During operation of the 2A EDG, the Diesel Area Ventilation will ensure the 2A EDG Room does not exceed a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> °F.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> For Part 1, the 2A EDG can be run at a MAXIMUM of 2750 KW for 2000 hours. For Part 2, during operation of the 2A EDG, the Diesel Area Ventilation will ensure the 2A EDG Room does not exceed a MAXIMUM of 120 °F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, 90 °F is close to the actual limit and is a reasonable distractor given the importance of the EDGs.</div>

For Part 1, 2600 KW is the continuous rating of the 2A EDG. For Part 2, 90 °F is close to the actual limit and is a reasonable distractor given the importance of the EDGs.</div>
