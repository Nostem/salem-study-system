<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Units 1 and 2 are at 100% Power<br>
&bull; John Doe is a licensed SRO who is also a qualified STA<br><br>
In accordance with Technical Specifications, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
John Doe <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 1 and Unit 2 concurrently.<br><br>
John Doe <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2 while concurrently filling the SRO minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2.<br><br>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW TS Table 6.2-1, while fulfilling the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2, John Doe may fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 1. For Part 2 and IAW TS Table 6.2-1, while fulfilling the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2, John Doe may concurrently fill the SRO minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that John Doe may not fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 1 and Unit 2 concurrently. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that John Doe may not fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 1 and Unit 2 concurrently. For Part 2, the candidate may incorrectly conclude that John Doe may not fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2 while concurrently filling the SRO minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate may incorrectly conclude that John Doe may not fill the STA minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2 while concurrently filling the SRO minimum staffing requirement on Unit 2.</div>
