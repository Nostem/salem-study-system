<strong>Given:</strong><br>
- Unit 1 is at 100% Reactor Power and stable<br>
- The crew is performing S1.OP-AB.RC-0002 (High Activity In Reactor Coolant System)<br>
- 11 Charging Pump is in service<br>
- 1CV4 (75 GPM ORIFICE) is in service<br>
- Chemistry has just reported that the confirmatory sample has confirmed increasing activity in the RCS but below LCO 3.4.9 (Specific Activity) limits<br><br>
In accordance with S1.OP-AB.RC-0002, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The crew <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> required to place the 13 Charging Pump in service and then secure the 11 Charging Pump.<br><br>
In addition to 1CV4, the crew will also place <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> in service.

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW S1.OP-AB.RC-0002 Step 3.14, the crew is required to have a Centrifugal Charging Pump (CCP) in service. Consequently, the crew is NOT required to place the 13 Charging Pump (PDP) in service then secure the 11 Charging Pump. For Part 2 and IAW S1.OP-AB.RC-0002 Step 3.16, the crew is required to maximize letdown flow. Consequently, in addition to 1CV4, the crew is required to also place 1CV3 (45 GPM ORIFICE) in service.</div>

For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the 13 Charging pump must be placed in service when performing S1.OP-AB.RC-0002. Part 2 is correct.</div>
