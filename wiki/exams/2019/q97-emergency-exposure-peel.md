In accordance with <span class="hi">NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0304</span> (OPERATIONAL SUPPORT CENTER (OSC) RADIATION PROTECTION RESPONSE), which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The Planned Emergency Exposure Limit (PEEL) for life saving is <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> REM.<br><br>
The EDO <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> can approve the emergency exposure.

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and IAW NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0304 (OPERATIONAL SUPPORT CENTER (OSC) RADIATION PROTECTION RESPONSE), the Planned Emergency Exposure Limit (PEEL) for life saving is 75 REM. For Part 2 and IAW NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0304, Emergency Coordinator authorization that requires Emergency Exposure for LIFE SAVING TASKS should be done verbally.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, 25 REM is Planned Emergency Exposure Limit (PEEL) for accident mitigation. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, 25 REM is Planned Emergency Exposure Limit (PEEL) for accident mitigation. For Part 2, since the SM served as the interim EDO, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the SM and EDO can approve the emergency exposure.</div>

Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, since the SM served as the interim EDO, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the SM and EDO can approve the emergency exposure.</div>
