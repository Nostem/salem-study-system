&bull; OHA B-2, "2A 125 VDC CNTRL BUS VOLT LO" actuates<br>
&bull; 2A Vital 125 VDC Bus voltage is reading 0 VDC on 2RP9<br><br>

1. #1 SGFP Emergency Oil Pump loses power<br>
2. Main Turbine Emergency Oil Pump loses power<br>
3. 2A EDG is NOT available for start<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
With a confirmed 2A Vital 125 VDC Bus voltage of 0 VDC, Unit 2 will experience equipment malfunction(s) ___
