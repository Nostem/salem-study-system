<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; The crew is performing S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 (LOSS OF CONDENSER VACUUM) due to a malfunction of the Condenser Air Removal system<br>
&bull; With Condenser Backpressure degrading, Unit 2 Turbine Load is being lowered in an attempt maintain condenser backpressure within the Allowable Operating Region of S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 Attachment 4, Condenser Absolute Pressure Limits<br><br>
At time 19:00<br>
&bull; Condenser Backpressure has just exceeded the limit of S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 Attachment 4<br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 45% Reactor Power<br><br>
At 19:00 and in accordance with S2.OP-AB.COND-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
The crew will TRIP the <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.<br>
After the Reactor/Turbine is tripped, the crew will <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> Reactor <span class="blank">(2)</span> perform S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 concurrently with 2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip Or Safety Injection</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> Turbine <span class="blank">(2)</span> perform S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 concurrently with S2.OP-AB.TRB-0001, Turbine Trip Below P-9</div>

<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and at 19:00, Reactor Power is 45% which is less than P-9. IAW S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 (LOSS OF CONDENSER VACUUM) CAS, the crew will trip the turbine when Condenser Backpressure exceeds the limit of S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 Attachment 4. For Part 2 and IAW S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 CAS, after the Turbine is tripped, the crew will perform S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 concurrently with S2.OP-AB.TRB-0001, Turbine Trip Below P-9.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, if Reactor Power had been above P-9, tripping the Reactor would have been correct IAW S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 CAS. For Part 2, transitioning to EOP-TRIP-1 and not continuing with S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 is correct if the reactor had been required to be tripped.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, if Reactor Power had been above P-9, tripping the Reactor would have been correct IAW S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 CAS. For Part 2, the candidate could have incorrectly concluded that performing EOP-TRIP-1 and S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 concurrently was required.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that performing S2.OP-AB.COND-0001 concurrently with S2.OP-AB.TRB-0001, Turbine Trip Below P-9 was not required.</div>
