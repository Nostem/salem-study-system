<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 3 preparing to do a plant startup<br>
&bull; The Electrical Systems Operator has notified Unit 2 that the Orchard 500 KV Line (5021) and the New Freedom 500KV Line (5024) will be unavailable starting at 15:00 for a duration of 6 hours<br><br>
At time 15:00<br>
&bull; Unit 2 has been isolated from the Orchard 500 KV Line (5021) and the New Freedom 500KV Line (5024)<br>
&bull; The 500 KV Salem-Hope Creek Tie-Line (5037) is still available to Unit 2<br><br>
Considering ONLY the following actions below:<br>
1. Demonstrate the OPERABILITY of the remaining independent A.C. circuit by performing Surveillance Requirement 4.8.1.1.1.a within 1 hour<br>
2. Demonstrate the OPERABILITY of three diesel generators by performing Surveillance Requirement 4.8.1.1.2.a.2 within 1 hour<br>
3. Immediately suspend all operations involving positive reactivity changes<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
At 15:00 and in accordance with Unit 2 LCO 3.8.1.1 (A.C. Sources - Operating), the crew must perform action(s) ___

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; A. Correct.</strong> Based on the question stem and at 15:00, the crew must perform LCO 3.8.1.1 Required Action A (With an independent A.C. circuit of the above required A.C. electrical power sources inoperable). The only 1 hour or less required action per LCO 3.8.1.1(a.1) is to demonstrate the OPERABILITY of the remaining independent A.C. circuit by performing Surveillance Requirement 4.8.1.1.1.a within 1 hour.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. LCO 3.8.1.1 and LCO 3.8.1.2 (ELECTRICAL POWER SYSTEMS SHUTDOWN) have required actions that are consistent with Actions 2 and 3 contained in the question stem. Action 2 would be required within 8 hours if two of the above required independent A.C. circuits inoperable (LCO 3.8.1.1 (D)). Action 3 would be required if the Unit was in Mode 5 &amp; 6 and with one of the above minimum required A.C. electrical power sources not OPERABLE. Consequently, any distractor containing Action 2 or Action 3 is plausible.</div>
