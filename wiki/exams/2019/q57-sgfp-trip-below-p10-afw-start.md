<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE: 054 Loss of MFW-AA2.03 (4.1)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 1 is at 8% Reactor Power<br>
&bull; 11 SGFP is in service<br>
&bull; 11 CN Pump and 12 CN Pump are in service<br><br>
At time 18:00<br>
&bull; 11 SGFP Trips<br>
&bull; ALL SG NR levels are 33% and lowering<br>
&bull; The crew enters S1.OP-AB.CN-0001 (MAIN FEEDWATER/CONDENSATE SYSTEM ABNORMALITY)<br><br>
In accordance with S1.OP-AB.CN-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The crew will ___
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> TRIP the Reactor AND GO TO 1-EOP-TRIP-1 (Reactor Trip or Safety Injection)</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> REDUCE Reactor Power to less than 4%, then START 11 and 12 AFW Pumps</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> REDUCE Reactor Power to less than 4%, then START 12 SGFP</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> IMMEDIATELY start 12 SGFP</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> Since Reactor Power is less than P-10 (10% Power), then IAW S1.OP-AB.CN-0001 Steps 3.2, 3.3, 3.14, 3.15, 3.16 and 3.17, the crew will REDUCE Reactor Power to less than 4%, then START 11 and 12 AFW Pump.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. There are multiple criteria in the S1.OP-AB.CN-0001 CAS that required a reactor trip. Consequently, the candidate could misapply the plant conditions to the CAS and incorrectly conclude that a Reactor Trip is required.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Since 12 SGFP is available and has a greater feed capacity than the AFW system, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the 12 SGFP should be started once Reactor power is lowered below 4%.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Since 12 SGFP is available and has a greater feed capacity than the AFW system and SG levels are lowering, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the 12 SGFP should be started immediately.</div>
