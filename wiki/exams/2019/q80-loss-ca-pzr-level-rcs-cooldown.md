<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 3 during a plant startup<br>
&bull; 22 Charging Pump is in service<br>
&bull; TAVG is 547 &deg;F<br>
&bull; Control Air Pressure is 84 psig and lowering<br>
&bull; PZR level is 25% and rising<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
Any time Pressurizer Level approaches a MINIMUM of <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>%, then the crew is required to commence an RCS Cooldown to 350 &deg;F using guidance contained in <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> Any time Pressurizer Level approaches a MINIMUM of 90%, then the crew is required to commence an RCS Cooldown to 350 &deg;F using guidance contained in S2.OP-AB.CA-0001.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> For Part 1, 70% is the PZR LEVEL HI Bezel Alarm. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude a cooldown to 350 &deg;F is required any time PZR Level approaches 70%. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> For Part 1, 70% is the PZR LEVEL HI Bezel Alarm. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude a cooldown to 350 &deg;F is required any time PZR Level approaches 70%. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.CA-0001 sends the crew to IOP S2.OP-IO.ZZ-0006 (HOT STANDBY TO COLD SHUTDOWN) for the cooldown.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.CA-0001 sends the crew to IOP S2.OP-IO.ZZ-0006 (HOT STANDBY TO COLD SHUTDOWN) for the cooldown.</div>
