<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">W/E12 Uncontrolled Depressurization of all Steam Generators-EK3.3 (3.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; A Reactor Trip and Safety Injection Actuation have occurred<br>
&bull; The 1C 4KV VITAL BUS is de-energized due to an electrical fault on the bus<br>
&bull; The 11 AFW Pump will NOT start<br>
&bull; The crew has just transitioned to 1-EOP-LOSC-2 (Multiple Steam Generator Depressurization)<br>
&bull; The RCS cooldown rate is 120 &deg;F / HR<br><br>
In accordance with 1-EOP-LOSC-2, which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
The crew <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> isolate steam to the 13 AFW Pump.<br><br>
AFW flow will be controlled / reduced to <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> (1) will &nbsp;&nbsp; (2) maintain SG NR Levels less than 33%</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> (1) will &nbsp;&nbsp; (2) no less than 1.0E04 LB/HR to each SG</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> (1) will NOT &nbsp;&nbsp; (2) no less than 1.0E04 LB/HR to each SG</div>
</div>

<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> For Part 1 and based on the question stem, ONLY 11 MDAFW Pump is not available. IAW 1-EOP-LOSC-2 Step 1, since the 13 AFW Pump is NOT the only source of AFW flow, steam will be isolated to the 13 AFW pump. For Part 2 and IAW 1-EOP-LOSC-2 Step 5, with RCS cooldown rate greater than 100 &deg;F / HR, AFW flow will be reduced to no less than 1.0E04 LB/HR to each SG.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, if the RCS cooldown rate was less than 100 &deg;F / HR, the crew will control AFW flow to maintain SG NR Levels less than 33%. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that with the given plant conditions, AFW flow will be controlled to maintain SG NR Levels less than 33%.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, if NO MDAFW Pump was available, then 1-EOP-LOSC-2 requires steam NOT to be isolated to the 13 AFW Pump. Based on the plant conditions, the candidate could not recognize that 12 AFW Pump is available. This would cause the candidate to incorrectly deduce that steam will NOT be isolated to the 13 AFW Pump. For Part 2, if the RCS cooldown rate was less than 100 &deg;F / HR, the crew will control AFW flow to maintain SG NR Levels less than 33%. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that with the given plant conditions, AFW flow will be controlled to maintain SG NR Levels less than 33%.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, if NO MDAFW Pump was available, then 1-EOP-LOSC-2 requires steam NOT to be isolated to the 13 AFW Pump. Based on the plant conditions, the candidate could not recognize that 12 AFW Pump is available. This would cause the candidate to incorrectly deduce that steam will NOT be isolated to the 13 AFW Pump. Part 2 is correct.</div>
