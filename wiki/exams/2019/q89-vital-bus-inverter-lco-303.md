<strong>REFERENCE PROVIDED</strong><br><br>
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 100% Reactor Power<br><br>
At time 09:00 on June 12<br>
&bull; The Vital Instrument Bus 2A Inverter is declared inoperable<br><br>
At time 09:30 on June 12<br>
&bull; The 2A Vital Instrument Bus is now being powered from its alternate power source<br><br>
At time 12:00 on June 12<br>
&bull; The Vital Instrument Bus 2D Inverter is declared inoperable<br><br>
At time 12:30 on June 12<br>
&bull; The 2D Vital Instrument Bus is now being powered from its alternate power source<br><br>
In accordance with Unit 2 Technical Specifications, which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
If BOTH Inverters remain inoperable, then ___ is the LATEST Time / Date that Unit 2 is required to be in HOT STANDBY.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 18:00 on June 12</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 19:00 on June 12</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 18:00 on June 13</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 18:00 on June 15</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> Based on the question stem, at 0900 on June 12, the crew must perform LCO 3.8.2.1 (Required Action A). At 1200 on June 12 and with 2 inverters now inoperable, the crew must now perform LCO 3.0.3 (since there is no REQUIRED ACTION of LCO 3.8.2.1 for 2 inoperable inverters). LCO 3.0.3 requires the Unit to be placed in HOT STANDBY within a maximum of 7 hours (1200 on June 12 + 7 hours = 1900 on June 12).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 18:00 on June 12 is the time/date if the candidate correctly determines that LCO 3.0.3 is applicable but incorrectly determines that LCO 3.0.3 allows only 6 hours to be in HOT STANDBY.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 18:00 on June 13 is the time/date to restore the inverter to OPERABLE status IAW LCO 3.8.2 REQUIRED ACTION B.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 18:00 on June 15 is the time/date to place the Unit in HOT STANDBY IAW LCO 3.8.2 REQUIRED ACTION B (if 2D Inverter remains inoperable).</div>
