<strong>Initial Conditions:</strong><br>
- Salem 1 is at 100% power.<br>
- A Locked Valve surveillance IAW S1.OP-ST.CAN-0001, Primary Containment Valves Monthly, is scheduled to be performed on your shift.<br>
- One of the valves to be verified is located in the Unit 1 Pipe Alley, Elevation 84 ft, Auxiliary Building.<br>
- An entry into the Pipe Alley has been made since the last performance of this surveillance requiring this valve position to be verified.<br><br>
<strong>Initiating Cue:</strong><br>
- You are the Unit 1 RO and have been assigned to perform the valve verification in the Unit 1 Pipe Alley.<br>
- Using the provided Radiological Survey Map and procedures, determine the following:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;1. What is the Radiological Posting for the area that the task will be performed?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;2. What is the highest On Contact radiation dose rate?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;3. What is the highest General Area dose rate?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;4. What protective clothing is required to enter this area?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;5. Calculate your personnel dose exposure. Assume the following:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- It will take 1 minute to complete the task<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Use the highest On Contact radiation dose rate as the General Area dose rate for the room

<div style="margin-top:12px;padding:10px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;">
<strong style="color:#22c55e;">Key Decision Point:</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">Step 5 is the discriminating step. The applicant must use the highest On Contact dose rate (205 mrem/hr) as the General Area dose rate per the initiating cue, then calculate the dose for a 1-minute exposure: 205 mrem/hr / 60 min/hr x 1 min = 3.42 mrem. The trap is to use the General Area dose rate (10 mrem/hr) instead of the On Contact rate as instructed. The HRA Briefing Sheet includes a dose rate alarm setpoint of 250 mrem/hr (increased from 205 to avoid conflict with the survey map dose rate) — the applicant should not confuse this alarm setpoint with the actual dose rate.</span>
</div>
