<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE: 025 Loss of RHRS-G2.1.27 (3.8)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 4<br>
&bull; 21 RHR Loop is in service<br>
&bull; PZR Level is 10% and stable<br><br>
At time 17:00<br>
&bull; The crew enters S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 (Loss Of RHR) due to a malfunction of 21RH18 (RHR HX FLOW CONT VALVE)<br><br>
At time 17:05 (5 minutes later)<br>
&bull; PZR level is 9% and lowering<br>
&bull; 2R11A (Containment Particulate Monitor) reading is rising<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
At 17:05, the crew will <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> IMMEDIATELY GO TO S2.OP-AB.LOCA-0001 (Shutdown LOCA)</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> PERFORM S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 Attachment 9 (Steam Generator Reflux Cooling)</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> IMMEDIATELY GO TO S2.OP-AB.RHR-0002 (Loss of RHR at Reduced Inventory)</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> CONTINUE in the main body of S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 (Loss Of RHR)</div>
</div>

Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 (Loss Of RHR) CAS, going to S2.OP-AB.LOCA-0001 (Shutdown LOCA) is correct since the Unit is in MODE 4 when the RCS Inventory was lost.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Performing S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 Attachment 9 (Steam Generator Reflux Cooling) would have been correct if at any time a complete loss of all vital buses occurs, NOT as a result of a Beyond-Design-Basis-External-Event (IAW S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 CAS).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Going to S2.OP-AB.RHR-0002 (Loss of RHR at Reduced Inventory) would have been correct if RCS had been aligned for operation &lt;101 ft. elevation (S2.OP-AB.RHR-0001 Steps 3.3 and 3.4).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> "START Safety Injection and Charging Pumps as required and CONTROL Pressurizer level between 5% and 50% while maintaining RHR System in service" would have been correct if the Unit was in MODE 5 or 6.</div>
