<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> With Rods in auto, control speed varies based on the total temperature error. With a temperature error of 1.5 °F to 3 °F, rods will move at 8 spm. With a temperature error of 3 °F to 5 °F, rods will move from 8 spm to 72 spm (linearly). This corresponds to an increase of 32 spm / °F. Consequently, with a TOTAL TEMPERATURE ERROR of 4 °F, rods will move 40 spm (8 spm + (32 spm / °F x 1 °F)).</div>
