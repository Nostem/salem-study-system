<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
&bull; Fuel Handling activities are in progress in the Fuel Handling Building<br><br>
At time 0900<br>
&bull; 2R32A (Fuel Handling Crane Radiation Monitor) fails high and the High Radiation Alarm for 2R32A actuates<br><br>
Considering ONLY the following actions:<br><br>
1. ALL crane motion is locked out<br>
2. ALL crane motion other than downward movement of suspended load is locked out<br>
3. FHB ventilation is transferred to HEPA and Charcoal Filters and FHB Exhaust Fans are started<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
With 2R32A failed high, ONLY Action(s) ___ will AUTOMATICALLY occur.
