• The crew enters S2.OP-AB.NIS-0001 (NUCLEAR INSTRUMENTATION SYSTEM MALFUNCTION)<br>
<br>
At time 1945:00<br>
• 2N41 is placed in tripped condition in accordance with S2.OP-SO.RPS-0001 (NUCLEAR INSTRUMENTATION CHANNEL TRIP / RESTORATION)<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br>
<br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.NIS-0001 and prior to placing 2N41 in tripped condition, the crew <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> REQUIRED to place the Rod Bank Selector Switch in MAN.<br>
