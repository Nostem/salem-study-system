<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
&bull; 21 Fuel Handling Area Exhaust Fan is in service<br>
&bull; 22 Fuel Handling Area Exhaust Fan is in standby<br>
&bull; 21 HEPA ONLY Filter Unit is in service<br>
&bull; HEPA 22 PLUS CHAR is in standby<br><br>
At time 17:00<br>
&bull; 2R9 (Fuel Storage Area Monitor) goes into alarm<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
Five Minutes later at 17:05, <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> Fuel Handling Area Exhaust Fan(s) and <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> Filter Unit(s) is/are in service.<br><br>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> When 2R9 goes into alarm, BOTH Fuel Handling Area Fans receive an auto start signal and 21 HEPA ONLY Filter Unit Isolates while 22 HEPA PLUS CHAR filter unit automatically aligns. Consequently, at 17:05, 21 and 22 Fuel Handling Area Fans and ONLY HEPA 22 PLUS CHAR Filter Unit are in service.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that only the running fan (21) will be in service at 17:05. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that only the running fan (21) will be in service at 17:05. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that BOTH HEPAs will be in service at 17:05.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that BOTH HEPAs will be in service at 17:05.</div>
