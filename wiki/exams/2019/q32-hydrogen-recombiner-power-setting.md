<span class="val-normal">REFERENCES PROVIDED</span><br><br>
&bull; A LOCA has occurred<br>
&bull; 22 Hydrogen Recombiner is inoperable<br>
&bull; The crew is preparing to place 21 Hydrogen Recombiner in service<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Containment Temperature PRIOR to the LOCA was 90&deg;F<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; CURRENT Containment Temperature is 120&deg;F<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; CURRENT Containment pressure as indicated on 2PI-948A is 4.0 psig<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-SO.CAN-0001 Attachment 1 and Attachment 2: Reference Power for 21 Hydrogen Recombiner is 43.68 KW. Power Correction Factor (Cp) per Attachment 2 (RECOMBINER POWER CORRECTION FACTOR CURVE) is 1.21. Power setting = Reference Power x Power Correction Factor (Cp) = 43.68 x 1.21 = 52.85 which is rounded to 54 KW.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 56 KW is the calculated setting if the candidate correctly used the 21 Hydrogen Recombiner Reference Power (43.68 KW) and incorrectly read Attachment 2 to determine a Power Correction Factor (Cp) of 1.24.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 52 KW is the calculated setting if the candidate incorrectly used the 22 Hydrogen Recombiner Reference Power (42.63 KW) and correctly read Attachment 2 to determine a Power Correction Factor (Cp) of 1.21.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. 50 KW is the calculated setting if the candidate correctly used the 21 Hydrogen Recombiner Reference Power (43.68 KW) and incorrectly read Attachment 2 to determine a Power Correction Factor (Cp) of 1.15.</div>
