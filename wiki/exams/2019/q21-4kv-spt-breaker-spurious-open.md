<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q21 — 4KV SPT Breaker Spurious Open</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">062 A.C. Electrical Distribution-K3.01 (3.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is MODE 3<br>
• 2A 4KV Vital Bus is powered from 23 SPT<br>
• 2B and 2C 4KV Vital Buses are powered from 24 SPT<br>
<br>
Subsequently<br>
• 23ASD (23 SPT Supply Breaker to 2A 4KV Vital Bus) SPURIOUSLY opens<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br>
<br>
After the electrical transient is complete, the 2A 4KV Vital Bus will be ___
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> de-energized</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> powered from 2A EDG with the blackout loads loaded by the 2A SEC</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> powered from the 24 SPT and the 2A EDG will NOT start</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> powered from the 24 SPT and the 2A EDG will start and remained unloaded</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> With 23 SPT at normal voltage when 23ASD spuriously opens, the 23SPT transfer relay remains energized and an AUTOMATIC transfer of the 2A 4KV Vital Bus to the 24 SPT will not occur. Consequently, the 2A 4KV Bus will de-energize and the 2A EDG will start and power the bus in accordance with the 2A SEC operating in Mode II*.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. Since the 23SPT transfer relay remains energized, the operator may incorrectly determine that the 24 SPT and 2A EDG transfer scheme is defeated causing the 2A 4KV Bus to remain de-energized.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. This is the expected result if the 23 SPT transfer relay had de-energized.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect but plausible. This would be partially correct if the 23 SPT transfer relay had de-energized.</div>
