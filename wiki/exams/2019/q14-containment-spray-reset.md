<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; An AUTOMATIC Safety Injection and Containment Spray Actuation have occurred<br>
&bull; A MANUAL backup of the Containment Spray Actuation has NOT been performed<br>
&bull; The crew is preparing to reset Containment Spray in accordance with 2-EOP-LOCA-1 (LOSS OF REACTOR COOLANT)<br>
<br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br>
<br>
The NCO will reset one train of Containment Spray by ___
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> depressing bezel button B ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> turning key switches A AND C in the clockwise direction 180 degrees ONLY</div>
