<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 100% Reactor Power and stable<br>
<br>
At time 09:00:00<br>
&bull; 22 SGFP trips<br>
<br>
At time 09:00:15 (15 seconds later)<br>
&bull; The crew enters S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 (MAIN FEEDWATER/CONDENSATE SYSTEM ABNORMALITY)<br>
&bull; Unit 2 is at 100% Reactor Power<br>
<br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.CN-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br>
<br>
The crew will initiate a MANUAL Main Turbine load reduction until less than or equal to a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> % Turbine Power at a rate less than or equal to a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> %/MIN.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> (1) 50 &emsp; (2) 30</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> (1) 50 &emsp; (2) 15</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> (1) 66 &emsp; (2) 30</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> (1) 66 &emsp; (2) 15</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.CN-0001, the crew will initiate a Main Turbine load reduction until less than or equal to a MAXIMUM of 66% Turbine Power at a rate less than or equal to a MAXIMUM of 15%/MIN.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> For Part 1, there are only two SGFPs. The candidate could incorrectly conclude that each SGFP provides 50% total flow. Consequently when 22 SGFP trips, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 requires a load reduction to 50% Turbine Power. For Part 2, a loss of stator cooling water will cause an automatic turbine runback at 30%/minute. Consequently, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the maximum turbine load ramp in S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 is 30%/MIN.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> For Part 1, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that each SGFP provides 50% total flow and S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 requires a load reduction to 50% Turbine Power. Part 2 is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is correct. For Part 2, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that the maximum turbine load ramp in S2.OP-AB.CN-0001 is 30%/MIN (confusing with the stator cooling water loss automatic runback rate).</div>
