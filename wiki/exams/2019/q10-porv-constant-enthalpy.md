- Pressurizer pressure is 415 psig and slowly lowering<br>
- There is a suspected leak by of 2PR1 (PZR PORV)<br>
- PRT pressure is 0 psig<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statements below?<br><br>
If 2PR1 is leaking by, then the expected PORV tailpipe temperature will be approximately ___ &deg;F.

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> Leak by of 2PR1 is a constant enthalpy expansion process through a valve. Using steam tables and with PZR pressure at 415 psig (430 psia), the h<sub>g</sub> is approximately 1205 BTU/lbm. Using the Mollier diagram, the throttling process will go from 415 psig to 0 psig with h<sub>g</sub> (1205 BTU/lbm) remaining constant. This will correspond to a tailpipe temperature of approximately 330°F.</div>
