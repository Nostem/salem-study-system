<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">000015AK3.01 (2.5)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
Which ONE of the following would be an indication of the potential for damage to a Reactor Coolant Pump and require tripping the Reactor Coolant Pump in accordance with S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, Reactor Coolant Pump Abnormality, Attachment 2, Stopping Reactor Coolant Pumps.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Closure of CC-131, RCP Thermal Barrier Valve for > 2 minutes.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Reactor Coolant Pump Motor Bearing Temperature of 170°F.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Reactor Coolant Pump Motor Flange Vibration of 3 mils.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Reactor Coolant Pump Motor Winding Temperature of 320°F.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> The Continuous Action Statement requiring stopping of RCPs is a Motor Winding temperature greater than 302°F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may remember the Continuous Action Statement that says if RCP Seal Injection Flow and RCP Thermal Barrier Component Cooling Water Flows are lost concurrently, then RCPs should be secured within 2 minutes to prevent RCP damage. Incorrect as RCP Seal Injection Flow is unaffected.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may believe the Continuous Action Statement requires stopping RCPs if Motor Bearing Temperature is greater than 170°F. Incorrect in that the CAS temperature is greater than 175°F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may remember that motor flange vibration of greater than 3 mils is an entry condition for AB.RCP-0001. Incorrect as the CAS requiring the stopping of RCPs is motor flange vibration greater than 5 mils.</div>
