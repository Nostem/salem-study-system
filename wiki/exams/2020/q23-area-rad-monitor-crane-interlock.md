<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
Which of the following interlocks are activated when Area Radiation Monitor 2R32A, Fuel Handling Crane fails to its' alarm setpoint?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> ALL crane hoist operation is prevented, but the hoist may be lowered after pressing the BYP INT pushbutton.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> FHB exhaust shifts to 22 HEPA plus Charcoal.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> ALL Crane trolley operation is prevented.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> ONLY Crane hoist-up operation is prevented</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> Only crane hoist-up operation is prevented. Conservative operation would allow the assembly to be lowered back into the spent fuel location (increased water shielding).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that all crane hoist operation is prevented and that a BYP INT pushbutton exists to defeat the interlock. Incorrect as there is no bypass interlock pushbutton and the alarm only prevents upward motion of the hoist.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may remember that two other area radiation monitors in the FHB (R5 &amp; R9) do cause the FHB exhaust filter to swap to 22 HEPA plus Charcoal. Incorrect as the R32A only affects the crane. Plausible that the candidate could feel the cause of an actual high alarm condition would cause R5 &amp; R9 to alarm as well. Incorrect, the stem states the monitor has failed to its alarm setpoint.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that all crane trolley operation is prevented. Incorrect as only the crane hoist-up operation is affected.</div>
