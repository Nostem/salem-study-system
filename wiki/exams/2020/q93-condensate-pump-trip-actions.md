<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• All Condensate Pumps are in service.<br>
• All Heater Drain Pumps are in service.<br>
• The Condensate Polisher is in service.<br>
<br>
Subsequently, 21 Condensate Pumps trips.<br>
<br>
Complete the following statement regarding the required actions the CRS will direct in accordance with S2.OP-AB.CN-0001(Q), Main Feedwater / Condensate System Abnormality;<br>
<br>
The CRS will direct the opening of the 21-23CN108, Polisher Bypass Valves if SGFP Suction Pressure is less than <span class="blank">___(1)___</span> and reduce Reactor Power to a MAXIMUM of <span class="blank">___(2)___</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> 265 psig<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> 75%</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> 320 psig<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> 75%</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> 320 psig<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> 85%</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> 265 psig<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> 85%</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> S2.OP-AB.CN-0001(Q), Main Feedwater/ Condensate System Abnormality directs the opening of 21-23CN108, Polisher Bypass Valves if SGFP suction pressure is less than 320 psig. S2.OP-AB.CN-0001(Q), Attachment 2 directs a power reduction to 85% or less.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> The first part is plausible because the CN47 will control in automatic to maintain a minimum of greater than 265 psig SGFP suction pressure. Incorrect as S2.OP-AB.CN-0001(Q), Main Feedwater/ Condensate System Abnormality directs their opening if SGFP suction pressure is less than 320 psig. The second part is plausible as 75% is the power level where the third condensate pump is started in accordance with IOP-4 during a power accession.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> The first part is correct. The second part is plausible as 75% is the power level where the third condensate pump is started in accordance with IOP-4 during a power accession.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> The first part is plausible because the CN47 will control in automatic to maintain a minimum of greater than 265 psig SGFP suction pressure. Incorrect as S2.OP-AB.CN-0001(Q), Main Feedwater/ Condensate System Abnormality directs their opening if SGFP suction pressure is less than 320 psig. The second part is correct.</div>
