<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
Unit 2 was initially operating at 100% Power when the following sequence of events occurs;<br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>The reactor trips.</li>
<li>A valid demand for Safety Injection occurs.</li>
<li>The crew enters 2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip or Safety Injection.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Subsequently, the following occurs;<br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>23 AFW Pump tripped on overspeed and cannot be reset due to trip throttle valve damage.</li>
<li>21 AFW Pump is tagged out for impeller replacement.</li>
<li>22 AFW Pump has tripped on motor overcurrent.</li>
<li>All Steam Generator NR Levels are &lt; 9%.</li>
<li>All Steam Generator Pressures are stable.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Which ONE of the following indicates the proper procedural usage of the Emergency Operating Procedures (EOPs)?<br><br>
Note: 2-EOP-FRHS-1, Loss of Secondary Heat Sink<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;CFST, Critical Safety Function Status Trees
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Immediately transition to 2-EOP-FRHS-1.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Complete all immediate actions of 2-EOP-TRIP-1, then transition to 2-EOP-FRHS-1.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Continue to perform 2-EOP-TRIP-1 until directed to transition to 2-EOP-FRHS-1.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Continue to perform 2-EOP-TRIP-1 until CFST monitoring is directed, then transition to 2-EOP-FRHS-1.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> Step 20 of EOP-TRIP-1 specifically directs the implementation of EOP-FRHS-1 when aux feed flow cannot be established.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that CFSTs are applicable as soon as EOP-TRIP-1 is initiated.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because OP-AA-101-111-1003, Use of Procedures states; "continuous required actions apply as soon as the immediate actions are verified." Incorrect as the TRIP-1 procedure CAS statements do not include a transition to FRHS-1.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible because OP-AA-101-111-1003, Use of Procedures states; "the functional restoration transitions apply only after EOP-TRIP-1 specifically directs the operator to begin monitoring the Critical Function Status Trees." This is step 30 of EOP-TRIP-1. Incorrect as both FRSM and FRHS both have specific CFST transitions before step 30.</div>
