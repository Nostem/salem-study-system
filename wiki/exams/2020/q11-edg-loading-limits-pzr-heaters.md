<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q11 — EDG Loading Limits for PZR Heaters</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">000056G2.1.25 (3.9)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
<br>
&bull; Unit 2 was at 100% Power when a Loss of Off-Site Power occurred.<br>
&bull; Operators have transitioned to 2-EOP-TRIP-2, Reactor Trip Response.<br>
&bull; All Emergency Diesel Generators are loaded onto their respective 4KV Vital Buses.<br>
<br>
The CRS has directed you to energize the 21 PZR Backup Heaters from the 2C Vital Bus in accordance with the station operating procedure.<br>
<br>
A procedural caution states the following; "Aligning pressurizer heaters to vital bus adds approximately 210 KW to bus load".<br>
<br>
The 2C diesel is currently loaded to the 2000 hour limit. In order to add the PZR heater load of 210 KW and NOT exceed the 2000 hour limit, the current 2C diesel loading must be adjusted to no greater than ______.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 2890 KW</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 2650 KW</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 2390 KW</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 2540 KW</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the candidate believes that the 2000 hour limit is 3100 Kw (actual 30 minute rating).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the candidate believes that the 2000 hour limit is 2860 Kw (actual 2 hour rating).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the candidate believes that the 2000 hour limit is 2600 Kw (actual Continuous rating).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> The station operating procedure, S2.OP-SO.PZR-0010, Pressurizer Backup Heaters Power Supply Transfer, states; "Maximum diesel generator load is 2750 KW (2000 hr rating)". 2750 - 210 = 2540 KW.</div>
