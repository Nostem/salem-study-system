<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 6.<br>
&bull; The Containment Equipment Hatch is closed.<br>
&bull; Core Reload is in progress.<br>
&bull; The transfer cart is in the Fuel Handling Building.<br>
&bull; A fuel assembly in the mast tube is in transit approaching the core.<br>
&bull; Gas bubbles are observed in the vicinity of the fuel assembly last placed in the core.<br>
&bull; The Refueling SRO has ordered all fuel transfers in progress stopped and all non-essential personnel evacuated from the containment.<br><br>
Based on the above conditions, complete the statement concerning the NEXT action taken in accordance with S2.OP-AB.FUEL-0001, Fuel Handling Incident:<br><br>
The CRS will direct placing the fuel assembly in the mast tube_________.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> in the upender and lower the upender to the horizontal position.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> into the core in any location that takes the least amount of time.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> into the core in the emergency location X-3.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> into the core in its designated location or the emergency location P-10, whichever is closer.</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> S2.OP-AB.FUEL-0001(Q), Fuel Handling Incident states; "Place the fuel assembly in the mast tube into the core in its designated location or the emergency location P-10 whichever is closer."</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the upender in the horizontal position is a safe position for a fuel assembly IAW AB.FUEL, if P-10 is not available or the assembly is indexed above the upender.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may believe that any core location is safe at this point of the fuel reload.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may confuse the safe location in the FHB (X-3) with the one in Containment (P-10).</div>
