<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">055000K3.01 (2.5)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>Unit 2 is at 100% Power.</li>
<li>The Plant Operator (PO) is swapping Condenser Vacuum Pumps in accordance with S2.OP-SO.AR-0001, Condenser Air Removal System Operation.</li>
<li>The PO has started 24 Vacuum Pump successfully and has just initiated a stop on 22 Vacuum Pump.</li>
<li>The 22AR25, Air Ejector Suction Isolation Valve, remains OPEN.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Which ONE of the following describes the status of main condenser vacuum and what procedural action the crew will take to mitigate the event?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Condenser backpressure is rising.  The PO restarts 22 Vacuum Pump.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Condenser backpressure is lowering.  The PO restarts 22 Vacuum Pump.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Condenser backpressure is rising.  The Secondary Field Operator ensures the 22AR27, Air Ejector Bypass Valve, is opened.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Condenser backpressure is lowering.  The Secondary Field Operator ensures the 22AR27, Air Ejector Bypass Valve, is opened.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> The operating procedural caution states the following; "Failure of applicable AIR INJECTOR SUCTION ISOLATION VALVE (AR25) to close when a Condenser Vacuum Pump is stopped will result in loss of condenser vacuum.  Contingency plans to attempt Vacuum Pump restart OR IMMEDIATE manual closure of the applicable AR23/AR25 valve should be considered whenever it is desired to maintain condenser vacuum when a Condenser Vacuum Pump is stopped.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> The first part is plausible as the candidate may confuse vacuum with backpressure, as vacuum will be lowering.  The second part is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> The first part is correct.  The second part is plausible because the candidate remembers that field operation is necessary and may believe that if the air ejector is bypassed with the vacuum pump stopped that any condenser vacuum loss will stop.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> The first part is plausible as the candidate may confuse vacuum with backpressure, as vacuum will be lowering.  The second part is plausible because the candidate remembers that field operation is necessary and may believe that if the air ejector is bypassed with the vacuum pump stopped that any condenser vacuum loss will stop.</div>
