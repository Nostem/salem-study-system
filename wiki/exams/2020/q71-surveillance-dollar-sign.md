<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q71 — Surveillance Dollar Sign ($) Significance</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">G2.2.12 Knowledge of surveillance procedures (3.7)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
When performing a Surveillance procedure, the operator encounters a step which has a dollar sign ( $ ) under the line where the operator initials completion of that step.<br><br>
What is the significance of the dollar sign ( $ )?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> It identifies an item required to meet Salem UFSAR acceptance criteria, which if not satisfactorily completed should be brought to the attention of the SM/CRS upon completion of the surveillance.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> It identifies an item required to meet Technical Specification acceptance criteria, which if not satisfactorily completed should be brought to the immediate attention of the SM/CRS.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> It identifies a step which requires Independent Verification of its completion PRIOR to continuing to the next step.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> It identifies a step which requires direct oversight by an assigned Reactivity Management SRO.</div>
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> All surveillance procedure precautions and limitations contain a step that states; "Steps identified with a dollar sign ($) are those items required to meet Technical Specification acceptance criteria. Such steps, if not satisfactorily completed, may have reportability requirements and are to be brought to the immediate attention of the SM/CRS."</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the candidate could believe that the $ identifies design or UFSAR acceptance criteria which could affect operability.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because the candidate could believe that the $ identifies a hold point requiring an Independent Verification prior to continuing to the next step.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible because the candidate could believe that the $ identifies a hold point requiring Reactivity Management SRO oversight.</div>
