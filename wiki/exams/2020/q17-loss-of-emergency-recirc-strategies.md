<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 has experienced a design bases Large Break LOCA coincident with a Loss of Off-Site Power.<br>
&bull; 2B Emergency Diesel Generator tripped.<br>
&bull; 21 RHR Pump tripped.<br>
&bull; The CRS has transitioned to 2-EOP-LOCA-5, Loss of Emergency Recirculation.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-LOCA-5, which of the following mitigation strategies are applicable based on CURRENT plant conditions and will NEED to be implemented?<br>
<br>
1. Run All CFCUs in High Speed.<br>
2. Minimize SI Flow to minimum for adequate decay heat removal.<br>
3. Depressurize the RCS to minimize RCS subcooling.<br>
4. Make up to the RWST.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 2, 3, and 4 Only.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 1 and 4 Only.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 2 and 4 Only.</div>
