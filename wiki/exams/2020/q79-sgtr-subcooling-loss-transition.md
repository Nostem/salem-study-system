<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q79 — SGTR-1 Subcooling Loss Transition to SGTR-3</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">000038EA2.07 (4.8)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• The crew is performing 2-EOP-SGTR-1, Steam Generator Tube Rupture.<br>
• Safety Injection has been terminated.<br>
• The crew has just restored normal charging alignment per 2-EOP-SGTR-1, step 30 when RCS subcooling lowers to 0 °F.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-SGTR-1, which ONE of the following completes the statement below:<br>
The crew will start ECCS pumps as necessary to restore subcooling and ___________.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> remain in 2-EOP-SGTR-1, Steam Generator Tube Rupture.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> go to 2-EOP-SGTR-2, Post SGTR Cooldown.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> go to 2-EOP-SGTR-3, SGTR with LOCA – Subcooled Recovery.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> go to 2-EOP-SGTR-4, SGTR with LOCA – Saturated Recovery.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> In accordance with 2-EOP-SGTR-1 CAS, "If SI has been terminated and RCS subcooling 0°F, then start ECCS pumps as necessary to restore subcooling and GO TO EOP-SGTR-3" (SGTR with LOCA – Subcooled Recovery).</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because based on plant conditions, the candidate could incorrectly conclude that remaining in EOP-SGTR-1 is required.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because transitioning to EOP-SGTR-2 is a possible transition from EOP-SGTR-1. However, this is not correct for given plant conditions.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that once subcooling has been lost, a transition to EOP-SGTR-4 (SGTR with LOCA – Saturated Recovery) is required.</div>
