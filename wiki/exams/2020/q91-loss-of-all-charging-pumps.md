<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q91 — Loss of All Charging Pumps</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">011A2.04 (3.7)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is in MODE 1 at approximately 8 % Power. Power ascension in progress.<br>
• 23 Charging Pump is cleared and tagged for maintenance.<br>
• 22 Charging Pump is in service.<br>
<br>
Subsequently;<br>
<br>
• 2C 4KV Bus de-energizes due to a differential fault on the bus.<br>
• The crew starts 21 Charging Pump, but it trips on overcurrent.<br>
<br>
Complete the following statement concerning the procedural action required to be taken.
The CRS will initiate <span class="blank">___________</span>.<br>
<br>
Note: S2.OP-AB.4KV-0003, Loss of 2C 4KV Vital Bus<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;S2.OP-AB.CVC-0001, Loss of Charging<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip or Safety Injection
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> S2.OP-AB.4KV-0003 to re-energize the 2C bus from the 2C Emergency Diesel Generator, then start 22 Charging Pump.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> S2.OP-AB.CVC-0001, to trip the Rx, confirm the Rx trip, initiate SI, and then enter 2-EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> S2.OP-AB.4KV-0003, to trip the reactor and then enter 2-EOP-TRIP-1</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> S2.OP-AB.CVC-0001, and direct Unit 1 to start 13 Charging Pump using Unit 1 RWST.</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ D. Correct.</strong> With no Unit 2 Charging Pumps available, step 3.50 of S2.OP-AB.CVC-0001(Q), Loss of Charging states; "COORDINATE with Unit 1 to place 13 Charging Pump in service using U/1 RWST."</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the S2.OP-AB.4KV-0003(Q) does verify that the 2C Bus is energized from the 2C EDG and then starts and stops 2C Vital Bus Loads as necessary. Incorrect as the bus is not energized from the EDG due to the bus differential fault.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because Tripping the Reactor and initiating Safety Injection is a possible action in S2.OP-AB.CVC-0001(Q) due to PZR Level not being able to be maintained. The candidate may believe that letdown is still in service and that PZR level is lowering in an uncontrolled manner.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because Tripping the Reactor is a possible CAS action in S2.OP-AB.4KV-0003(Q). The candidate may believe that it is a conservative action due to the 2C bus being deenergized and no pressurizer level control.</div>
