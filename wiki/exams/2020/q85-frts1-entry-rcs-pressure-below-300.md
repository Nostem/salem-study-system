<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">00WE08EA2.2 (4.1)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 3.<br>
&bull; RCS Temperature is 547&deg;F<br>
&bull; RCS Pressure is 2235 psig.<br><br>
Subsequently a LOCA occurs and the crew has transitioned from 2-EOP-TRIP-1, Reactor Trip or Safety Injection;<br><br>
&bull; RCS Pressure is 125 psig.<br>
&bull; RCS CETs read 380&deg;F.<br>
&bull; RCS Cold Leg temperatures are 250&deg;F.<br>
&bull; The RCS has cooled down &gt; 100&deg;F in the last 30 minutes.<br>
&bull; 22 RHR Pump failed to start.<br>
&bull; 21 RHR Pump is running providing 1150 gpm cold leg injection flow.
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
Based on the above conditions, complete the following statement;<br><br>
Entry into 2-EOP-FRTS-1, RESPONSE TO IMMINENT PRESSURIZED THERMAL SHOCK CONDITIONS is &hellip;
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> made but NO actions are implemented before returning to procedure in effect.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> NOT required since RCS pressure is below 350 psig.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> made and a RCS temperature soak for a ONE hour period will be completed.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> NOT required since S2.OP-AB.LOCA-0001, SHUTDOWN LOCA, will address any thermal shock concerns.</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; A. Correct.</strong> 2-EOP-FRTS-1 is entered due to a PURPLE path on the CFSTs (RCS Cooldown &gt; 100&deg;F/hr, RCS T-colds &gt; 230&deg;F but &lt; 280&deg;F). The RCS Pressure Status step (1) then determines that RCS pressure is &lt; 300 psig and that RHR flow is at least 300 gpm, directing a return to procedure in effect.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may believe that because RCS pressure is less than 350 psig a transition to 2-EOP-FRTS-1 is not required. Incorrect as although the first step in FRTS reviews RCS pressure &lt; 300 psig and RHR flow &ge; 300 gpm, entry into the procedure is still required due to the PURPLE path on the CFSTs.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may remember 2-EOP-FRTS-1 requires a one-hour soak if the RCS cooldown has exceeded 100&deg;F / hr. Incorrect as step 1 directs a return to the procedure in effect.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the candidate may believe that since the plant started in MODE 3 that AB.LOCA-0001 would be the appropriate mitigating procedure. Incorrect as AB.LOCA is used during MODE 4 or MODE 3 with the accumulators isolated and neither of those conditions existed in the stem. The EOP network is appropriately entered in MODE 3, at normal operating pressure.</div>
