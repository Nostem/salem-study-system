<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">075000K2.02 (2.5)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>Unit 2 are at 100% Power.</li>
<li>21A Circulator is out of service (C/T) for Waterbox cleaning.</li>
<li>23B Circulator is out of service (C/T) for Traveling Screen replacement.</li>
<li>A Liquid Waste release is in progress from 21 CVCS Monitor Tank to the 21 SW Header.</li>
</ul>
<br>
The following sequence of events occurs:<br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>21B Circulator trips.</li>
<li>The crew enters S2.OP-AB.CW-0001, Circulating Water System Malfunction.</li>
<li>Field operator makes an error in throttling hotwell isolation valves resulting in 21A and 21B Hotwell levels at 30 inches.</li>
<li>21 Condensate Pump amps are oscillating.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Based on the above conditions, which one of the following describes the next required actions and the MAXIMUM power level allowed in accordance with station procedures?<br><br>
<em>Note:  S2.OP-AB.CN-0001, Main Feedwater / Condensate System Abnormality<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;S2.OP-AB.LOAD-0001, Rapid Load Reduction</em>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Stop 21 Condensate Pump and reduce reactor power to ≤ 85% in accordance with S2.OP-AB.CN-0001.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Stop 21 Condensate Pump and reduce reactor power to ≤ 83% in accordance with S2.OP-AB.LOAD-0001, to prevent flashing in Condensate System.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Terminate the liquid release, stop 21 Condensate Pump, and reduce reactor power to ≤ 85% in accordance with S2.OP-AB.CN-0001.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Terminate the liquid release, stop 21 Condensate Pump, and reduce reactor power to ≤ 83% in accordance with S2.OP-AB.LOAD-0001, to prevent flashing in Condensate System.</div>
</div>

Correct.</strong> Step 3.10 states; "Initiate a load reduction to less than or equal to 83% Reactor Power, IAW S2.OP-AB.LOAD-0001(Q), Rapid Load Reduction to prevent flashing in Condensate System."</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible but incorrect because although reduction in reactor power to a maximum of 85% is stated in AB-CN-0001, AB.CW-0001 would require a reduction to a maximum of 83%.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible as the candidate may believe that the liquid release path is to the 21A&B Circulating Pump discharge.  Incorrect the liquid release via 21 CCW HX is directed to the Unit 1 Circulating Water (11A&B) Pump discharge.  Remainder is incorrect for the same reasons as A above.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible as the candidate may believe that the liquid release path is to the 21A&B Circulating Pump discharge.  Incorrect the liquid release via 21 CCW HX is directed to the Unit 1 Circulating Water (11A&B) Pump discharge.  The second part is correct.</div>
