<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 has experienced a Small Break LOCA with a Loss of Off-Site Power.<br>
• A fault developed on the 2A 4KV Bus and the Bus is de-energized.<br>
• All available ECCS Pumps are currently running.<br>
• Containment Pressure is currently 4.3 psig.<br>
• RCS Pressure is currently 1035 psig.<br>
• The crew has transitioned to 2-EOP-LOCA-2, Post LOCA Cooldown and Depressurization.<br>
• The crew is implementing "Charging Pump Reduction" major action of EOP-LOCA-2.<br>
• Based on operability concerns with the Subcooling Margin Monitor, the CRS has directed the RO to determine subcooling requirements using Steam Tables.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following choices is the MAXIMUM RCS (Core Exit) temperature that will allow stopping one Charging Pump in accordance with step 21 of 2-EOP-LOCA-2?<br><br>
[REFERENCES PROVIDED]
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 500°F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 415°F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 409°F</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 407°F</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> Only one SI Pump is running, all RCPs are stopped, and Containment Adverse conditions exist per the question stem. The required subcooling is 141°F from Table C. Using steam tables, the T-Sat for 1035 psig (1049.7 psia) is 550.56°F. Therefore the max core exit temperature that will allow stopping one Charging Pump is 409°F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because candidate may believe two SI Pumps are running and choose a required subcooling of 135°F from Table C. Incorrect because only one SI Pump is running due to the fault on 2A 4Kv Bus.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because candidate may use the normal containment value and choose a required subcooling of 50°F from Table C. Incorrect because containment pressure is 4.3 psig and therefore adverse numbers apply.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because candidate may incorrectly use 1035 psig instead of 1049.7 psia. Using 1035 psia, the candidate incorrectly calculates T-Sat as 548.83°F.</div>
