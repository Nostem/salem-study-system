<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
&bull; Unit 2 is in MODE 4.<br>
&bull; Personnel performing work in Containment notify the Control Room of a large Service Water Cooler leak on 22 CFCU.<br>
&bull; The crew has entered S2.OP-AB.SW-0001, Loss of Service Water Header Pressure.<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.SW-0001, complete the following statements concerning isolating a service water leak on a CFCU:<br>
(1) The CRS will direct stopping 22 CFCU and isolating the SW leak by closing the <span class="blank">____(1)___</span>.<br>
(2) The basis for this action is to <span class="blank">___(2)___</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> SW58 (Inlet Water Valve) first and then the SW72 (Outlet Water Valve).<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> allow closing the SW54 (CFCU SW Inlet) and SW76 (CFCU SW Outlet) in the field with a lower differential pressure across the valves.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> SW72 (Outlet Water Valve) first and then the SW58 (Inlet Water Valve).<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> allow closing the SW54 (CFCU SW Inlet) and SW76 (CFCU SW Outlet) in the field with a lower differential pressure across the valves.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> SW58 (Inlet Water Valve) first and then the SW72 (Outlet Water Valve).<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> minimize the possibility of water hammer following restoration.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">(1)</span> SW72 (Outlet Water Valve) first and then the SW58 (Inlet Water Valve).<br><span class="blank">(2)</span> minimize the possibility of water hammer following restoration.</div>
