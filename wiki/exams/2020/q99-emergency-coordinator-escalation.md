<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
An event has occurred on Salem Unit 1:<br>
<ul style="margin:0;padding-left:20px;">
<li>The TSC is ACTIVATED.</li>
<li>The EOF is MANNED and NOT ACTIVATED.</li>
</ul>
<br>
In accordance with NC.EP-EP.ZZ-0102, Emergency Coordinator Response, which ONE of the following describes the individual responsible for escalating an emergency event level from a Site Area Emergency (SAE) to a General Emergency (GE)?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> The Shift Manager.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> The Emergency Duty Officer.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> The Emergency Response Manager.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> The Site Vice President.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> With the TSC activated, the Emergency Coordinator responsibilities shift to the EDO.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because if the TSC was not activated, the Shift Manager would be responsible. Plausible because the Shift Manager is responsible for making all emergency status change announcements in the control room. Incorrect as the TSC is activated.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because if both the TSC and EOF were activated, the Emergency Response Manager would be the Emergency Coordinator. Incorrect as the EOF is not activated.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may remember that the Site Vice President may be assigned to an Emergency Plan Position, including the Emergency Response Manager. Incorrect as the position is filled with a number of senior management individuals. The authority is based on the individual's E-Plan position, not his management title.</div>
