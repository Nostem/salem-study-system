<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q20 — Misaligned Rods Required Action</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE05AA1.04 (3.9)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
&bull; Unit 2 was initially at 100% Power.<br>
<br>
Subsequently, the following sequence of events occurs:<br>
<br>
&bull; Automatic Turbine runback occurs due to an issue with the stator water cooling system.<br>
&bull; During the load reduction, the RO reports that two (2) Control Bank D rods have stopped moving at 215 steps.<br>
&bull; The CRS enters S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001, Immovable / Misaligned Control Rods.<br>
&bull; The RO has placed the Rod Bank Selector Switch in MANUAL.<br>
&bull; Control Bank D Group Demand counters are presently at 185 steps.<br>
&bull; The runback terminates with reactor power at 80 %.<br>
&bull; Tavg is being maintained within +/- 1.5 &deg;F of program.<br>
<br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001, and assuming the two control rods will not be restored to operable status, which ONE of the following describes a required action that the crew will take.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Place the Unit in Hot Shutdown.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Reduce power to &lt; 50 % rated thermal power.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Place the Unit in Hot Standby.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Reduce power to &lt; 75 % rated thermal power.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001(Q), Immovable / Misaligned Control Rods directs placing the unit in Hot Standby if more than one rod is stuck / misaligned.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that the procedure directs placing the unit in Hot Shutdown vice the actual directed Hot Standby.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Plausible because the procedure, S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001(Q), Immovable / Misaligned Control Rods, directs reviewing QPTR based on the misaligned rod/rods. If QPTR Technical Specification limits were exceeded, Tech Spec 3.2.4 requires reducing power to less than 50% if the QPTR is not returned to within limits after 24 hours. Incorrect as the procedure specifically directs placing the unit in Hot Standby if more than one rod is stuck / misaligned.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Plausible because this is true if only one control rod was stuck / misaligned. Incorrect because the stem states two rods are misaligned.</div>
