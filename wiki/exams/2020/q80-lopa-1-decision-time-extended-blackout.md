<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• A Loss of All AC Power occurs.<br>
• The crew has entered 2-EOP-LOPA-1, Loss of All AC.<br>
• Maintenance has reported that all three EDGs have been extensively damaged and restoration cannot be expected for at least 12 hours.<br>
• The Load Dispatcher has informed the Shift Manager that restoration of Offsite Power cannot be expected for at least 24 hours.<br>
<br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-LOPA-1, the CRS must make a decision within <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> if AC power sources will be restored within the appropriate time requirements to support the SBO licensing basis, and then based on this decision the CRS will <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
