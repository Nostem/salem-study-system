<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
Which ONE of the following lists the correct operational plant parameters used as input to the OT&#916;T reactor protection setpoint calculation?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Tavg, Reactor Power, and &#916;T only.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Tavg and &#916;T only.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Tavg, Pressurizer Pressure, and &#916;I only.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Tavg and Pressurizer Pressure only.</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> OT&#916;T is a DNB protection trip and uses Tavg, Pressure, and delta flux as input values.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may believe that reactor power is an input to the OT&#916;T setpoint calculation to establish a rated or nominal &#916;T value to be used in the calculation.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because the candidate may confuse the inputs for OP&#916;T with those for OT&#916;T. Candidate may believe that over-temperature only includes temperature inputs.</div>

Delta flux is set to zero for OP&#916;T, not OT&#916;T.</div>
