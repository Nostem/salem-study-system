<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q92 — Containment Hydrogen Concentration</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">028G2.1.20 (4.6)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• The Unit 2 crew is performing 2-EOP-LOCA-1, Loss of Reactor Coolant, Step 24, "Containment Hydrogen Concentration."<br>
• Containment Hydrogen Concentration is 2.1 %.
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
In accordance with 2-EOP-LOCA-1, which of the following describes the required procedural action(s)?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Start ONLY one Hydrogen Recombiner.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Start BOTH Hydrogen Recombiners.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Continue in 2-EOP-LOCA-1 until Containment Hydrogen concentration reaches 4.0%.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Consult TSC for additional recovery actions and continue in 2-EOP-LOCA-1.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer & Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> IAW 2-EOP-LOCA-1, if hydrogen concentration is between 0.5% and 4.0%, then only one hydrogen recombiner is started.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Plausible because the operating procedure would start two recombiners if hydrogen concentration was 2.0% and rising. Candidate may also believe that the EOP starts both recombiners.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Plausible because 4.0% is a decision parameter used in EOP-LOCA-1, step 24. Incorrect because concentration less than 4.0% results in the procedure directing the start of one recombiner.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Plausible because consulting the TSC would have been the correct answer if hydrogen concentration was ≥ 4.0%.</div>
