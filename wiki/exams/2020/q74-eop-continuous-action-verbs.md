<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
In accordance with <span class="hi">OP-AA-101-111-1003, Use of Procedures</span>, Salem EOPs' Continuous Action Steps are either surrounded by a shaded box or contain which of the following continuous action verbs?<br><br>
1. ADJUST<br>
2. CONTROL<br>
3. MAINTAIN<br>
4. MODIFY<br>
5. MONITOR<br>
6. VERIFY
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 2, 3, 5 ONLY.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 3, 4, 6 ONLY.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 1, 5, 6 ONLY.</div>
