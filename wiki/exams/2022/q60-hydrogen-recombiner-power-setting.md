<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• A LOCA has occurred<br>
• 21 Hydrogen Recombiner is inoperable<br>
• The crew is preparing to place 22 Hydrogen Recombiner in service<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;○ Containment Temperature PRIOR to the LOCA was 90°F<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;○ Containment Temperature is currently 120°F<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;○ Containment pressure peaked at 10 psig<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;○ Containment pressure is currently 5 psig as indicated on 2PI-948A<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes the statement below?<br><br>
The crew will set the 22 Hydrogen Recombiner Power Setting to _____ KW.<br><br>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-SO.CAN-0001, Attachment 1 and 2, for 22 Hydrogen Recombiner the power setting is calculated as follows: Power Correction factor (Cp) x Reference Power = Recombiner Power Setting; Cp at 5 psig and 90°F (Cp=1.24) and Reference Power = 42.63 KW; therefore Power Setting = 1.24 x 42.63 = 52.86 KW; per step 5 of Attachment 1 round off to next higher setting which can be read on meter which would be 54 KW.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> This is power setting if you use Cp at 10 psig and 90°F (Cp=1.425). 1.425 x 42.63 = 60.74 KW or rounded off the next higher setting would be 62 KW.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> This is power setting if you use Cp at 10 psig and 120°F (Cp=1.4). 1.4 x 42.63 = 59.68 KW or rounded off the next higher setting would be 60 KW.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> This is power setting if you use Cp at 5 psig and 120°F (Cp=1.21). 1.21 x 42.63 = 51.58 KW or rounded off the next higher setting would be 52 KW.</div>
