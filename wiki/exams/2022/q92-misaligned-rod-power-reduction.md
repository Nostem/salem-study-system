<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q92 — Misaligned Rod Power Reduction</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">014 A2.04 (3.9)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• Rod Control Exercise Testing is in progress per S2.OP-ST.RCS-0001, Reactivity Control System Rod Control Assemblies.<br>
• After withdrawing Control Bank C rods to the ARO position, the RO reports the following;<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Group Demand Counter for Control Bank C indicates 230 steps.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- ARPI indication on P-250 indicates one Control Bank C rod at 215 steps.<br>
• The crew enters S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001, Immoveable/Misaligned Control Rods.<br>
• Reactor Engineering confirms the misaligned rod.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following describes the required action per S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Insert Control Bank C to 215 steps to realign the bank to the misaligned control rod.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Reduce power to less than 75% Rated Thermal Power.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Place the unit in HOT STANDBY within 6 hours.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Power operation at 100% may continue, provided shutdown margin is satisfied.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001 step 3.37, ONLY one rod indicates misalignment 12 or more steps if greater than 85% RTP, then step 3.41 refers to evaluate TS (including TS 3.1.3.1 movable control assemblies) and step 3.42 directs initiating power reduction to &lt; 75% RTP to comply with TS 3.1.3.1 action c.3.d.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. This is not allowed per S2.OP-AB.ROD-0001. If the rod is to be recovered, then the rods are aligned to the current bank position. Credible distractor if the SRO believes that moving the bank to the misaligned rods is allowed by procedure, which actually the procedure aligns the misaligned rod to the bank.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. This action is for two misaligned rods. Credible distractor because it is a step in the procedure but for two or more misaligned control rods.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. This action is required by TS 3.1.3.1 action c.3, but it is not the only action required. The unit must be reduced to less than 75% RTP as well to comply with TS 3.1.3.1 action c.3.d.</div>
