<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Both Salem Units are operating at 100% Power.<br>
• Hope Creek is operating at 100% Power.<br>
• Fire Brigade current manning consists of 6 qualified personnel.<br>
• During a training exercise on-site, two members of the Fire Brigade are injured and require off-site transportation to Salem Memorial Hospital.<br><br>
In accordance with the Salem FSAR, which ONE of the following completes both statements?<br><br>
1. The MINIMUM staffing requirements of the fire brigade <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> MET.<br>
2. The fire brigade staffing may be less than the minimum required for a period of time not to exceed <span class="blank">_(2)_</span> hour(s) provided immediate action is taken to restore the fire brigade to minimum requirements.
</div>
