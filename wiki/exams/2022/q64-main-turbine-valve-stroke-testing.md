<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is at 89% Power.<br>
• Main Turbine Valve testing is in progress in accordance with S2.OP-PT.TRB-0003, Main Turbine Valve Stroke Testing.<br><br>
In accordance with S2.OP-PT.TRB-0003, which ONE of the following completes the statement?<br><br>
While performing the valve stroke test, if a <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> valve does NOT reopen, then the operator is required to <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 1) Reheat or Intercept<br>2) trip the reactor</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 1) Governor or Stop<br>2) reduce turbine load to &lt; 75% within an hour</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 1) Governor or Stop<br>2) trip the reactor</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-PT.TRB-0003, P&amp;L step 3.12.3, except for short periods during testing, if any HP turbine inlet valve (MS28/MS29) closes and does not reopen or must be isolated by the operator, then turbine load should be reduced to 75% within one hour.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the operator believes that the requirement to reduce power to 75% within one hour is for a Reheat or Intercept valve. First part is incorrect. IAW S2.OP-PT.TRB-0003 P&amp;L step 3.12.6, except for short periods during testing, the Main Turbine should NOT be operated above 80% load with a Reheat Stop Valve or Intercept Valve closed. Do Not exceed 5 minutes with any valve closed before beginning to reduce load at 10%/hr. Continue load reduction until either the valve is open or less than 80% load is achieved. Second part is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the operator believes that the requirement to reduce power to 75% within one hour is for a Reheat or Intercept valve. First part is incorrect. IAW S2.OP-PT.TRB-0003, P&amp;L step 3.12.3, except for short periods during testing, if any HP turbine inlet valve (MS28/MS29) closes and does not reopen or must be isolated by the operator, then turbine load should be reduced to 75% within one hour. Second part is incorrect. Reactor trip is not required by procedure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible if the operator believes that a reactor trip is required when a Governor or Stop valve is closed. See answer C for further explanation.</div>
