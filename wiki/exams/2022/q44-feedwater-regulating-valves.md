<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q44 — Feedwater Isolation on SG Hi-Hi Level</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">059 K4.19 (3.2)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Reactor Power.<br><br>
Subsequently, the following occurs:<br>
• A malfunction with 21BF19 causes the SG level to rise uncontrollably.<br>
• The reactor automatically trips.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following describes the expected plant response?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> BOTH SGFPs trip and the BF13s, BF19s, and BF40s close.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> BOTH SGFPs trip and ONLY the BF19s and BF40s close.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> ONLY the BF19s and BF40s close.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> ONLY the BF13s, BF19s and BF40s close.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; A. Correct.</strong> SG HI-HI Level Rx Trip (P-14, 2/3 NR levels on 1 SG > 67%) will actuate Feedwater Isolation that will trip the Main Turbine and both SGFPs, and then close all the BF13s, BF19s, and BF40s.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the applicant may confuse the valves that close on a Feedwater Interlock which are the BF19s and BF40s only.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. The BF19s and BF40s will ONLY close when there is a Feedwater Interlock signal (P-4 and Tavg &lt; 554 °F). In this case, a P-14 signal was generated from a SG level HI-HI resulting in a FW Isolation signal.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because the applicant may confuse the actions of a Feedwater Isolation with Feedwater Interlock and that the Main Turbine and SGFPs will not trip on a Feedwater Isolation.</div>
