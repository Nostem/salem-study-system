<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q8 — CCW / RCP Motor Bearing Temp</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">APE 26 AA2.04 (2.5)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• The RO reports the following:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- CCW Surge Tank Levels are lowering slowly.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- 2DR107, Surge Tank Make-up Valve, is opened and Surge Tank level is stable at 42 %.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- OHA A-41, AUX ALM SYS PRINTER, annunciates for Containment Sump Pump start then stops 5 seconds later.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- The RO reports 23 RCP motor bearing temperatures are 160 °F and rising at 0.5 °F per minute.<br>
• The crew enters S2.OP-AB.CC-0001, Component Cooling Abnormality.<br><br>
Based on current plant conditions and in accordance with S2.OP-AB.CC-0001, which ONE of the following is the MAXIMUM time allowed before tripping the reactor and stopping the RCP?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 15 minutes</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 20 minutes</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 25 minutes</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 35 minutes</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">▶ Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ C. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.CC-0001 Attachment 1, reactor trip and stopping affected RCP is required when motor bearing temperature reaches 175 °F. At 25 minutes RCP bearing temperature will be 172.5 °F which is still below the 175 °F limit. Note: S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 Attachment 1 uses motor bearing temperature greater than 175 °F.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> At 15 minutes the temperature will be 167.5 °F which is still below the 175 °F limit.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> At 20 minutes the temperature will be 170 °F which is still below the 175 °F limit.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> At 35 minutes the temperature will be 177.5 °F which has exceeded the 175 °F limit.</div>
