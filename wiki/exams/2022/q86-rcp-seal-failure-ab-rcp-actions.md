<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• The RO reports the following for 21 RCP:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Console Alarm, SEAL LEAK-OFF FLOW HI-LO, is LIT.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Seal Leak-off recorder indicates > 6 gpm.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Seal Injection Flow indicates 8.5 gpm.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Seal Inlet and Outlet Temperatures are indicating 109°F and rising.<br>
• The crew enters S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, Reactor Coolant Pump Abnormality.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes both statements?<br><br>
1. Which RCP seal failed?<br>
2. In accordance with S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001, what actions will the CRS direct the crew?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 1) #1<br>2) Perform an orderly shutdown and stop 21 RCP within 8 hours.</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ A. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.RCP-0001 Attachment 1, #1 seal leak-off flow greater or equal to 6 gpm then go to Attachment 2, Stopping RCPs. Attachment 2 will direct a manual reactor trip, stopping 21 RCP, then between 3-5 minutes after stopping RCP close the 21CV104 and go to 2-EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ B.</strong> Incorrect. First part is correct. Second part is incorrect. This action will be performed at step 3.6 if RCP seal leak-off flow is less than 0.8 gpm or is slowly approaching 6 gpm. Credible distractor since this is an action in AB.RCP-1 for a degraded #2 seal.</div>

High seal leak-off flow is indicative of a #1 seal failure. Second part is correct. Plausible because this is an action if it meets the RCP trip criteria per the AB.RCP.</div>

High seal leak-off flow is indicative of a #1 seal failure. Second part is incorrect. This action will be performed at step 3.6 if RCP seal leak-off flow is less than 0.8 gpm or is slowly approaching 6 gpm. Credible distractor since this is an action in AB.RCP-1 for a degraded #2 seal.</div>
