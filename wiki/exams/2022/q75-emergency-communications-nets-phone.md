<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• An ALERT has been declared on Unit 2.<br>
• You are the Primary Communicator.<br>
• You are directed to perform the State and Local notifications.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following is the preferred or primary communication method?<br><br>
Note: NETS – Nuclear Emergency Telecommunications System<br>
NAWAS – North America Warning Alert System (DE only)<br>
EMRAD – Emergency Radio (NJ only)<br>
ESSX-1 – PSEG microwave phone system
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> NETS phone</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Satellite phone</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; C. Correct.</strong> IAW EP-SA-325-F6, Primary Communicator Log, and NEPOVERVIEW, EP Overview, the primary communicator will use the NETS phone first, then use either ESSX-1 or DID system phones, then NAWAS radio for DE and EMRAD radio for NJ.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible because use of radios (NAWAS and EMRAD) is used as the backup to NETS.</div>

Plausible because the ESSX-1 is a microwave private phone system operated by PSEG and serves as the secondary means to the NETS phones.</div>
