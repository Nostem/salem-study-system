<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">008 A2.02 (3.2)</span>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is at 100% Power.<br>
• Console bezel alarm, SURGE TANK LEVEL HI-LO, is LIT.<br>
• CCW Surge Tank Level Channels A and B are LOWERING rapidly.<br>
• The crew is implementing S2.OP-AB.CC-0001, Component Cooling Abnormality.<br>
<br>
In accordance with S2.OP-AB.CC-0001, which ONE of the following completes the statement assuming the surge tank levels continue to lower?<br>
<br>
When Surge Tank levels lowers to less than <span class="blank">_(1)_</span>, the operator is required to trip the reactor and <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> 17%<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span class="blank">_(2)_</span> stop all RCPs, isolate Letdown, and swap Charging pump suction to the RWST</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> 17%<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span class="blank">_(2)_</span> stop all RCPs ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> 5%<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span class="blank">_(2)_</span> stop all RCPs ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> 5%<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span class="blank">_(2)_</span> stop all RCPs, isolate Letdown, and swap Charging pump suction to the RWST</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#x25B6; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#x2713; D. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.CC-0001 Attachment 1, if surge tank level can NOT be maintained greater than 5%, then stop RCPs IAW Attachment 2. Per Attachment 2, the Rx is tripped, all RCPs are stopped, letdown is isolated, charging pump suctions are swapped to the RWST and then go to EOP-TRIP-1.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#x2717; A.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. Surge tank level less than 5% requires stopping RCPs IAW Attachment 2. Second part is correct. Plausible since the 17% level is expected knowledge to the operators to take immediate actions associated with low VCT level.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#x2717; B.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. Surge tank level less than 5% requires stopping RCPs IAW Attachment 2. Second part is incorrect. Per Attachment 2, if loss of CCW was the cause of the event, then in addition to stopping RCPs, letdown needs to be isolated and the charging pumps suction swapped over to the RWST. Plausible since the 17% level is expected knowledge to the operators to take immediate actions associated with low VCT level.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#x2717; C.</strong> Incorrect. First part is correct. Second part is incorrect. Per Attachment 2, if loss of CCW was the cause of the event, then in addition to stopping RCPs, letdown needs to be isolated and the charging pumps suction swapped over to the RWST.</div>
