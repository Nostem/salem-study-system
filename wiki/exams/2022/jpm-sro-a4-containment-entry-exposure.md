<strong>Location:</strong> Classroom<br>
<strong>Time-Critical:</strong> No<br>
<strong>Alternate Path:</strong> No<br>
<strong>Estimated Time:</strong> 20 minutes<br><br>
<strong>Initial Conditions:</strong><br>
- Salem 2 is at 100% power reducing power at 10% per hour to perform Turbine valve testing.<br>
- The 22 CFCU has been declared inoperable.<br>
- Tagging has been completed.<br><br>
<strong>Initiating Cue:</strong><br>
- You are the WCCS and need to send operators into containment to remove tags to allow work on 22 CFCU disassembly to continue.<br>
- Operator A has 1992 mrem TEDE for the year and Operator B has 1496 mrem TEDE for the year so far.<br>
- Using the provided Radiological Survey Map and procedures, determine the following:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;1. Whose authorization outside of Operations, by position, is needed to enter the containment during the downpower?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;2. In accordance with the provided survey map perform the following:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2.1. Calculate the dose for each operator (Assume both operators each take 12 minutes in the area to perform their tagging evolution):<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Gamma dose ___ mrem<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Neutron dose ___ mrem<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2.2. Will the operators exceed the Administrative Annual Dose limit? If so, identify which Operator(s)?
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #34d399;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:14px;font-size:13px;">
<strong style="color:#34d399;">Task Standard:</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">1. Determines the Radiation Protection Supervisor must authorize entry.<br>2. Determines Gamma dose for each operator is 1 mrem.<br>3. Determines Neutron dose for each operator is 8 mrem.<br>4. Determines Operator A total dose is 2001 mrem and Operator B total dose is 1505 mrem. Operator A exceeds administrative dose control limit of 2000 mrem.</span>
</div>

<td style="padding:6px;color:#c8cdd5;"><span class="hi-exam">Determine if operators exceed Administrative Annual Dose limit and identify which operator(s)</span></td>
<td style="padding:6px;color:#c8cdd5;"><span class="hi-exam">Operator A: 1992 + 1 + 8 = 2001 mrem — EXCEEDS ADCL of 2000 mrem (1 mrem above).<br>Operator B: 1496 + 1 + 8 = 1505 mrem — below ADCL (495 mrem below).<br>Only Operator A exceeds the administrative dose control limit.</span></td>

<div style="margin-top:12px;padding:10px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;">
<strong style="color:#22c55e;">Key Decision Point:</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">Step 2.2 is the discriminating step — the applicant must add BOTH gamma (1 mrem) and neutron (8 mrem) doses to each operator's existing annual TEDE, then compare against the 2000 mrem Administrative Dose Control Limit. Operator A at 1992 + 9 = 2001 mrem just barely exceeds the limit by 1 mrem. The applicant must correctly include neutron dose (which is the dominant contributor at 40 mrem/hr vs 5 mrem/hr gamma) in the total to recognize the exceedance.</span>
</div>
