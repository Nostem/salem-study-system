<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 30% Power and stable.<br>
• ALL Steam Generator Narrow Range levels are 45%.<br><br>
Based on the above condition, which ONE of the following completes both statements?<br><br>
1. Steam Generator Narrow Range levels are <span class="blank">__(1)__</span> program setpoint.<br>
2. Assuming SGFP speed remains constant, to return Steam Generator levels to program setpoint, the demand on the BF19's will automatically <span class="blank">__(2)__</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 1) above<br>2) decrease</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 1) above<br>2) increase</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 1) below<br>2) decrease</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 1) below<br>2) increase</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; A. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.CN-0001, Attachment 2, SG program level with power above 20% is 44% NR level. With a SG NR level at 45%, the actual SG NR level is above the program setpoint of 44%. The digital feedwater system will lower the demands to the BF19s to throttle the valves closed to reduce feedwater flow with SGFP speed remaining constant.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. First part is correct. Second part is incorrect. The BF19 demand will lower to throttle the valves closed more to reduce feed flow to lower SG levels. Plausible in that the applicant may incorrectly believe that the operation of the BF19 works opposite to the demand signal.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. SG program level above 20% is 44%. Plausible because the operator may incorrectly recall the proper level setpoint for digital feedwater. Second part is correct.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. SG program level above 20% is 44%. Plausible because the operator may incorrectly recall the proper level setpoint for digital feedwater and how the BF19s should respond. Second part is incorrect. The BF19 demand will lower to throttle the valves closed more to reduce feed flow to lower SG levels.</div>
