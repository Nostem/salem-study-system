<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is in MODE 5.<br>
• A Containment Purge is in progress in accordance with S2.OP-SO.WG-0006, Containment Purge to Plant Vent.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes both statements concerning Containment Purge administrative requirements?<br><br>
1. A temporary termination of Containment Purge of short duration <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> permitted without the need for a new effluent permit.<br>
2. Reinstatement of Containment Purge must be authorized by the <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">✓ B. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-SO.WG-0006, P&amp;L step 3.2, a temporary termination of Containment Purge, without the need for a new effluent permit, is acceptable provided that the temporary termination is of short duration; for example approximately 4 hours, AND there is operational assurance that no radiological changes to the containment environment has occurred. Per Attachment 2, reinstatement of purge requires SM or CRS approval.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Part 1 is correct. Part 2 is incorrect. Plausible because Rad Pro Supervision authorizations are required for other activities involving radiation release or exposure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Part 1 is incorrect. Part 2 is incorrect. Plausible because Rad Pro Supervision authorizations are required for other activities involving radiation release or exposure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ D.</strong> Part 1 is incorrect. Plausible because the SRO may believe that a new effluent permit will be required anytime the purge is terminated. Part 2 is correct.</div>
