<span style="color:#60a5fa;font-size:14px;font-weight:600;">Q43 — Steam Dump Response to PT-507 Failure</span>
<span style="font-size:10px;padding:2px 8px;background:#0d1220;color:#6b7280;border-radius:3px;">039 A4.07 (2.8)</span>
</div>

<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br>
• Unit 2 is at 15% Power with a startup in progress.<br>
• Tavg is 552 °F and stable.<br>
• Steam Dumps are in AUTO MS PRESS control mode set to 980 psig.<br>
• 2PT-507, Steam Header Pressure transmitter, fails LOW.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following describes how the steam dump system will respond?<br><br>
The steam dump valves will <span class="blank">__(1)__</span> and the steam generator pressure will IMMEDIATELY <span class="blank">__(2)__</span>.
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 1) CLOSE<br>2) LOWER due to the reactor adding less heat to the RCS.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 1) CLOSE<br>2) RISE due to less heat removal from the RCS.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 1) OPEN<br>2) LOWER due to more heat removal from the RCS.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 1) OPEN<br>2) RISE due to the reactor adding more heat the RCS.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> 2PT-507 failing low will cause the steam dumps to close. When the steam dumps close, steam flow from the SGs will lower, Rx power will lower, and SG pressure will immediately rise due to less heat removal from the SGs until SG pressure is maintained by the MS10s opening.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. First part is correct. Second part is incorrect. SG pressure will rise due to steam flows lowering and less heat removal from the SGs. Plausible because the operator may incorrectly determine that SG pressure will lower when steam flow decreases.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. Steam dumps will close on failure of 2PT-507. Second part is incorrect. SG pressure will rise due to steam flows lowering and less heat removal from the SGs.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; D.</strong> Incorrect. First part is incorrect. Steam dumps will close on failure of 2PT-507. Plausible because the operator may incorrectly determine how the steam dumps will respond on the instrument failure. Second part is plausible because the applicant may incorrectly determine that with the steam dumps open, reactor power will increase and also increase the RCS temperature (incorrect assumption) which will result in more heat transfer to the SG and a rise in SG pressure.</div>
