<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is at 85% Power following a rapid load reduction.<br>
• OHA E-8, ROD INSERT LMT LO, is now LIT.<br>
• Tavg is 571°F and stable.<br>
• Tref is 570°F and stable.<br><br>
Which ONE of the following completes both statements?<br><br>
1. The Control Rod Insertion Limit of the Core Operating Limits Report (COLR) <span class="blank">_(1)_</span> been exceeded.<br>
2. Per the OHA Alarm Response Procedure for E-8, the NEXT action that the operating crew is required to perform is <span class="blank">_(2)_</span>.
</div>

<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; B. Correct.</strong> OHA E-8 alarms when the rods are 10 steps from the Rod Insertion Limit. Therefore, the RIL for the COLR has not been exceeded. IAW the OHA alarm response procedure, the crew is required to initiate a normal boration IAW S2.OP-SO.CVC-0006 to restore rods to their normal band.</div>
