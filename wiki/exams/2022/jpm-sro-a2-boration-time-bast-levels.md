<strong>Location:</strong> Classroom<br>
<strong>Time-Critical:</strong> No<br>
<strong>Alternate Path:</strong> No<br>
<strong>Estimated Time:</strong> 10 minutes<br><br>
<strong>Initial Conditions:</strong><br>
- Unit 2 experienced an automatic Reactor Trip from an inadvertent Main Turbine Trip.<br>
- 2SJ2 was tagged for emergent repairs prior to the trip.<br>
- SI is not required and the crew is implementing 2-EOP-TRIP-2, Reactor Trip Response.<br>
- Three Control Rods from Control Bank Delta have failed to FULLY insert.<br>
- RWST Concentration is 2350 ppm, and BAST concentration is 6650 ppm.<br>
- Unit 2 remains in Mode 3 throughout the boration.<br>
- Current BAST levels:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;21 BAST level: 70%<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;22 BAST level: 70%<br><br>
<strong>Initiating Cue:</strong><br>
- You are the CRS.<br>
- Determine the amount of time Rapid Boration is required IAW 2-EOP-TRIP-2 Step 4 for the Three Control rods that have failed to insert on Control Bank Delta:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;1. How many total minutes of Rapid Boration is required?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;2. What will be the final BAST levels in percent IAW S2.OP-TM.ZZ-0002?<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Assume the Rapid Boration flowrate remains at 40 GPM for the entire duration required in question 1, and Both BASTs lower equal amounts.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;3. Based on plant conditions after completion of the boration, identify any Tech spec(s) and Tech Spec action statement(s) required related to the boric acid system.
</div>

<div style="padding:10px 14px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #34d399;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:14px;font-size:13px;">
<strong style="color:#34d399;">Task Standard:</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">1. Calculates the total boration time for 3 stuck control rods to be 105 minutes.<br>2. Determines final BAST tank levels for 21 tank at 43% +/-2% and 22 tank at 43% +/-2%.<br>3. Determines that TS LCO 3.1.2.6.a.1 is not met and action is required.</span>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:12px;padding:10px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;">
<strong style="color:#22c55e;">Key Decision Point:</strong><br>
<span style="color:#c8cdd5;">Step 3 is the discriminating step — after completing the boration calculations, the applicant must recognize that the final BAST levels (43% each, 86% combined) fall below the required level from TS 3.1.2.6 Figure 3.1-2 (> 96.5% at the given RWST/BAST boron concentrations). This requires the applicant to enter the TS figure with both the RWST concentration (2350 ppm) and BAST concentration (6650 ppm) to determine the minimum required level, then compare it against the post-boration tank levels. The applicant must then identify the correct LCO and action statement.</span>
</div>
