<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
Which of the following choices identifies potential sources of RCS INLEAKAGE into the Component Cooling System?<br><br>
1. Seal Water Heat Exchanger<br>
2. CCW Heat Exchangers<br>
3. Regenerative Heat Exchanger<br>
4. Spent Fuel Pool Heat Exchanger<br>
5. RCP Thermal Barrier Heat Exchanger
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> 4 and 5 ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> 1 and 2 ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> 2 and 3 ONLY</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> 1 and 5 ONLY</div>
</div>

<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> IAW S2.OP-AB.CC-0001 Step 3.10, the RCP Thermal Barriers, Letdown HX, and Seal Water HX are all sources of RCS inleakage into the CCW system.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Incorrect. A leak at the spent fuel pool HX would cause out leakage of the CCW system. Plausible since a leak in the thermal barrier would cause RCS leakage into CCW.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible since a leak in the CCW HX would result in leakage into the CCW but it would be Service Water and not RCS.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible since the CCW HX would result in leakage into CCW, but CCW HX is SW leakage into RCS. In addition, the applicant may confuse the operation of the Regen HX and believe that the discharge of charging would leak into CCW which CCW does not cool the Regen HX, but letdown flow does.</div>
