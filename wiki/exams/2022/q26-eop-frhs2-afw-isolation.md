<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
<strong>Given:</strong><br><br>
• Unit 2 is implementing 2-EOP-FRHS-2, Response to Steam Generator Overpressure, due to high pressure in 21 Steam Generator.<br>
• The crew is at Caution step C6-1 that reads "AFW FLOW SHOULD REMAIN ISOLATED TO AFFECTED SG(s) UNTIL A STEAM RELEASE PATH IS ESTABLISHED".<br><br>
In accordance with 2-EOP-FRHS-2, which ONE of the following is the reason for this step?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Operation of the Turbine Driven AFW Pump at high steam pressure may cause turbine damage.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> AFW is a high pressure system which may cause further overpressure in a SG with no steam release path.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Differential temperatures between the existing high pressure conditions in the SG and the cold AFW will cause tube failure due to thermal stress.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> AFW pumps would operate near their shutoff head which could result in pump overheating or damage.</div>
</div>

Correct.</strong> IAW 2-EOP-FRHS-2, step C6-1 bases, if AFW flow is reestablished to an affected SG prior to establishing a steam release path, the AFW flow could further increase the affected SG pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ A.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible the 23 AFW Pump is used as a means to vent the SG(s) and the candidate may believe that high pressure in the affected SG could cause damage to the turbine blades.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">✗ C.</strong> Incorrect. Plausible since this bases is used on other FRHS procedures but it is not the reason for this action in FRHS-2.</div>
