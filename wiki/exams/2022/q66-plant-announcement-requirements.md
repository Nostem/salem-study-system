<div style="font-size:13px;color:#9ca3af;margin-bottom:14px;line-height:1.7;">
In accordance with OP-AA-104-101, Communications, which ONE of the following evolutions would require a plant announcement?
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom:14px;">
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>A.</strong> Swapping a 28 VDC battery charger to the standby charger.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>B.</strong> Commencing a fill of the Main Generator bulk hydrogen bottles from a vendor supply truck.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>C.</strong> Commencing a Containment Pressure Relief to lower Containment pressure.</div>
<div style="padding:10px 14px;border-left:3px solid #1a2035;margin-bottom:4px;font-size:12px;color:#c8cdd5;background:#0d1220;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;"><strong>D.</strong> Performing radiography in the Turbine Building.</div>
</div>

<details>
<summary style="cursor:pointer;color:#60a5fa;font-size:12px;font-weight:600;padding:8px 0;">&#9654; Show Answer &amp; Explanation</summary>
<div style="margin-top:10px;padding:16px;background:#0a0e17;border:1px solid #1a2035;border-radius:6px;font-size:12px;">
<div style="padding:8px 12px;background:#052e16;border-left:3px solid #22c55e;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:10px;color:#c8cdd5;"><strong style="color:#22c55e;">&#10003; D. Correct.</strong> IAW OP-AA-104-101, Communications, step 4.1.3.6, states in part the following; make public address announcements prior to activities that could change plant radiation levels. In this case, radiography performed in the Turbine Building will change radiation levels in the area and can expose personnel in the area to high radiation fields. Radiography performed in the Turbine Building are typically performed during refueling outages.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; A.</strong> Plausible because the operator may believe that this evolution meets the requirement for energizing of major electrical switchgear and buses. Swapping 28 VDC battery chargers does not meet the requirements of OP-AA-104-101 for page announcements.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; B.</strong> Plausible because the operator may believe that this evolution meets the requirements for alerting plant personnel of an unexpected event or condition. The candidate may believe that since Chemical off-loading at the polisher is announced, that off-loading Hydrogen would also be announced. Filling bulk hydrogen bottles does not meet the requirements of OP-AA-104-101 for page announcements.</div>
<div style="padding:8px 12px;border-left:3px solid #f87171;border-radius:0 4px 4px 0;margin-bottom:6px;color:#9ca3af;"><strong style="color:#f87171;">&#10007; C.</strong> Plausible because the operator may believe that this evolution meets the requirements for alerting plant personnel of an unexpected event or condition or possibly changing radiation levels in the plant. Containment pressure relief does not meet the requirements of OP-AA-104-101 for page announcements.</div>
