# EOP-LOPA-4 — Extended Loss of All AC Power

## Purpose

Provides operator actions for responding to an extended station blackout when AC power restoration cannot be expected within the SBO licensing basis coping time.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q80</div>
Per EOP-LOPA-1 step 29: <span class="hi-exam">the decision to transition to EOP-LOPA-4 must be made within 1 hour of the Loss of All AC Power</span>. Step 29.1: <span class="hi-exam">if AC Power cannot be restored within 4 hours, then go to EOP-LOPA-4</span>. <span class="hi-trap">The 1-hour time limit is for making the DECISION, not for the transition itself. The 4-hour criterion is whether AC power can be restored — if not, transition to LOPA-4. Do not confuse: the candidate does NOT have 4 hours to decide.</span>
</div>
