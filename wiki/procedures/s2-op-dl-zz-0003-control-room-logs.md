# S2.OP-DL.ZZ-0003 — Control Room Logs

## Purpose

Provides direction for recording control room logs during Modes 1-4, including channel checks for rod position indication, IRPI, and plant computer comparisons.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q56</div>
For Control Rod Counter channel checks, <span class="hi-exam">either the Group Demand Counter or Plant Computer will satisfy the surveillance for Group 1 only</span>. Only the Group 1 Demand Steps input into the Plant Computer. TS 3.1.3.2.1.b bases states: "either the control console group 1 demand step counter or the plant computer 'bank step' display is sufficient to comply with this specification for group 1 rod position."
</div>

<div class="callout callout-jpm">
<div class="callout-label">JPM — 2018 RO-A2</div>
Control room log 1830 readings: Tavg Channel Check for all 4 RC loops (must be within <span class="hi-exam">3 degrees F</span>, S/R 4.3.2.1.1 Modes 1-3) and OTDT setpoint check per REM FIGURE 5 (A-D). 2CC1 console OTDT readings compared against REM setpoint band. Loop 24 OTDT setpoint of 72 deg F is <span class="hi-exam">outside the REM FIGURE 5D setpoint band</span> — record UNSAT or red-circle and notify CRS.
</div>
