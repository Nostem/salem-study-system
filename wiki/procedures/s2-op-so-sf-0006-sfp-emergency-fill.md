# S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 — Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill

## Purpose

Provides direction for emergency makeup to the Spent Fuel Pool when normal makeup methods are insufficient or unavailable.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2018 Q92</div>
Per S2.OP-AB.SF-0001, the crew can perform <span class="hi-exam">S2.OP-SO.SF-0006 (Spent Fuel Pool Emergency Fill) OR S2.OP-SO.SF-0001 (Fill and Transfer of the Spent Fuel Pool)</span> to raise SFP level during a loss of SFP cooling event with the pool boiling and level at the Low Level alarm setpoint.
</div>
