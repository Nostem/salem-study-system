# S2.OP-SO.AR-0001 — Condenser Air Removal System Operation

## Purpose

Provides instructions for normal operation of the Condenser Air Removal System, including starting and stopping condenser vacuum pumps and swapping between vacuum pumps.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q63</div>
Operating procedural caution: <span class="hi-exam">"Failure of applicable AIR INJECTOR SUCTION ISOLATION VALVE (AR25) to close when a Condenser Vacuum Pump is stopped will result in loss of condenser vacuum."</span> Contingency plans to attempt vacuum pump restart OR IMMEDIATE manual closure of the applicable AR23/AR25 valve should be considered whenever it is desired to maintain condenser vacuum when a Condenser Vacuum Pump is stopped. <span class="hi-trap">If the AR25 remains OPEN with the vacuum pump stopped, condenser backpressure rises (vacuum lowers). The correct mitigation is to restart the vacuum pump — not to open the AR27 (Air Ejector Bypass Valve).</span>
</div>
