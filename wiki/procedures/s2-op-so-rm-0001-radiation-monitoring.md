# S2.OP-SO.RM-0001 — Radiation Monitoring System Operation

## Purpose

System operating procedure for the radiation monitoring system, covering normal operation and monitoring of plant radiation monitors.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q13</div>
R13 radiation monitors monitor CFCU Service Water discharge. CFCU Service Water discharges to the opposite unit's Circulating Water system.
