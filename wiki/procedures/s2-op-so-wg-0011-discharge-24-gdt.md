# S2.OP-SO.WG-0011 — Discharge of 24 Gas Decay Tank to Plant Vent

> DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides instructions for releasing radioactive waste gas from 24 Waste Gas Decay Tank (GDT) through the plant vent system.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2019 Q35</div>
During a 24 GDT release, <span class="hi-exam">2R41 Channel D High Radiation Alarm causes 2WG41 (GDT VENT CONT V) to AUTOMATICALLY close</span>, terminating the release. Other valves open during the release (24WG34, 2WG39, 2WG38) do NOT automatically close on 2R41 high radiation — <span class="hi-trap">only 2WG41 receives the automatic closure signal from 2R41</span>.
</div>
