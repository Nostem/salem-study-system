# S1.OP-SO.WG-0010 — Discharge of 13 Gas Decay Tank to Plant Vent

> DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides instructions for releasing radioactive waste gas from Unit 1 13 Waste Gas Decay Tank (WGDT) through the plant vent system. Covers Chemistry sampling and authorization, pre-release verification, CRS authorization, release initiation, monitoring, and termination.

## Key Actions / Information

### Pre-Release Verification (Attachment 1, Section 1.0)

Pre-Release verification in Attachment 1, Section 1.0 must be completed prior to CRS review for release authorization (before step 5.2.9).

### Maximum Allowable Release Rate (Step 3.4)

Per NOTE in Step 3.4: tanks with a calculated maximum allowable release rate of <= 32 SCFM **cannot be released**. The rate must be > 32 SCFM.

### CRS Authorization (Step 5.2.9)

CRS reviews all completed sections for accuracy and completeness before authorizing the gas release. Both the pre-release verification AND the calculated maximum allowable release rate must be satisfactory.
