# S2.OP-SO.RC-0002 — Vacuum Refill of the RCS

## Purpose

System operating procedure for vacuum refill of the RCS and establishing a pressurizer steam bubble.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2019 Q7</div>
