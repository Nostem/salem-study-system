# 460/230V AC

## Function

Steps down 4160V vital bus power via unit substations to feed motors, MOVs, heaters, and smaller loads throughout the plant. (UFSAR 8.3.1.3)

## 460V/230V Systems

- Step-down from 4160V vital buses via unit substations
- Feed motors, MOVs, heaters, and smaller loads
- Pressurizer heater buses (EP and GP) backed by EDGs for natural circulation support
(UFSAR 8.3.1.3)
