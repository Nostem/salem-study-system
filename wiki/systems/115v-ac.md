# 115V AC

## Function

Vital instrument buses provide regulated AC power to protection system instrumentation. Fed from inverters under normal operation with automatic transfer to regulated AC supply as backup. (UFSAR 8.3.1.4)

## 115V AC Instrumentation Power

- Vital instrument buses provide power to protection system instrumentation
- Fed from inverters (normal) with automatic transfer to regulated AC supply (backup)
(UFSAR 8.3.1.4)

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q12</div>
Loss of 2A 115V Vital Instrument Bus affects VCT level instrument LT-112 (loss of indication, alarms, and auto makeup). LT-114 remains available but <span class="hi-exam">only via the plant computer</span> in the control room (NOT on the control console) and at Panel 216 in charging pump alley.
</div>

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2020 Q88</div>
Loss of 2B VIB impact on ESFAS: Containment Spray Hi-Hi Containment Pressure bistables are <span class="hi-exam">energized to actuate</span>. Loss of the 2B VIB causes the associated bistable to remain de-energized → <span class="hi-exam">logic goes from 2/4 to 2/3</span>. Per TS 3.3.2.1, the inoperable channel is <span class="hi-exam">BYPASSED (not tripped)</span> to prevent spurious CS actuation. <span class="hi-trap">Most ESF bistables are de-energized to trip → tripped on inoperability. CS bistables are the exception — energized to actuate → bypassed on inoperability.</span>
</div>
