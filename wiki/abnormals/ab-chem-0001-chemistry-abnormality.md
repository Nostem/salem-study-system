# AB.CHEM-0001 — Secondary Chemistry Abnormality

## Purpose

Provides operator actions for responding to secondary chemistry abnormalities, including condenser tube leaks and gross tube failures.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2023 Q60</div>
A gross Condenser Tube Failure/Leak is indicated by rapidly rising Secondary Plant Chemistry recorder trends, 2CC1 and OHA alarms. It is defined as: <span class="hi-exam">30 ppb Condensate Pump Disch sodium with condensate polisher in service, OR 2 ppb Condensate Pump Disch sodium with condensate polisher out of service</span>, OR a rapidly increasing Condensate Pump discharge sodium expected to exceed those limits. Alarms confirming a CW waterbox tube leak: <span class="hi-exam">Dissolved Oxygen Hi, Condensate Pump Disch Sodium Hi, and Hotwell Outlet Conductivity Hi</span>. Hydrazine Lo is NOT expected from a CW tube leak.
</div>
