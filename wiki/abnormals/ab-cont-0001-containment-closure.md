# AB.CONT-0001 — Containment Closure

> ⚠️ DRAFT — Reconstructed from exam materials

## Purpose

Provides guidance for establishing containment closure during shutdown conditions when containment integrity is required but not maintained (e.g., during outages with open penetrations).

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2018 Q84</div>
AB.CONT-0001 containment closure timing depends on the initiating event:<br>
- <span class="hi-exam">Loss of RHR or Fuel Handling Incident: Containment Closure should be established prior to Core Boiling</span><br>
- <span class="hi-exam">Other initiating events: Containment Closure should be established within four hours</span><br><br>
Outage Equipment Hatch closure method also depends on the event:<br>
- <span class="hi-exam">Loss of RHR: may close using 4 corner bolts per AB.CONT-0001</span><br>
- <span class="hi-exam">Other events: install using ALL 10 bolts per SC.MD-FR.CAN-0001</span><br>
<span class="hi-trap">Trap: candidates may confuse the 4-corner-bolt expedited closure (Loss of RHR) with the full 10-bolt installation (non-RHR events). The 4-bolt option exists because time to core boiling may be limited during a Loss of RHR.</span>
</div>
