# AB.FUEL-0002 — Loss of Refueling Cavity or SFP Level

## Purpose

Provides operator guidance for responding to a loss of water level in the refueling cavity or the spent fuel pool.

## Key Actions / Information

<div class="callout callout-exam">
<div class="callout-label">Exam — 2018 Q34</div>
The Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve <span class="hi-exam">can ONLY be fully closed when the fuel transfer cart is inside containment</span>. The fuel transfer cart has two interlocks for cart movement: <span class="hi-exam">the upender must be in the horizontal position AND the Fuel Transfer Tube Gate Valve must indicate open (via the open limit switch)</span>. With a failed limit switch (no open indication), the cart can only be moved MANUALLY (not electrically). <span class="hi-trap">If the cart is in the Fuel Handling Building and refueling canal level is lowering, the crew cannot fully close the gate valve until the cart is moved inside containment.</span>
</div>
